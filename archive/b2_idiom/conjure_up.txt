# Key:conjure up
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The magician was able to <u>conjure up</u> a rabbit out of his hat.
E-2: The scent of freshly baked bread can <u>conjure up</u> feelings of nostalgia.
E-3: The book was able to <u>conjure up</u> a vivid image of the fantastical world it portrayed.

J-3: 本はファンタジーの世界を生き生きと描写してくれたので、私たちに<u>鮮明なイメージを想起させること</u>ができました。
