# Key:two sides of the same coin
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Some people say that love and hate are <u>two sides of the same coin</u>.
E-2: Success and failure are often <u>two sides of the same coin</u>.
E-3: Freedom and responsibility are <u>two sides of the same coin</u>.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 愛と憎しみは同じコインの<u>表裏</u>だという人もいます。
J-2: 成功と失敗はしばしば同じコインの<u>表裏</u>です。
J-3: 自由と責任は同じコインの<u>表裏</u>です。
