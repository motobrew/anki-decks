# Key:fiddly
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The small buttons on this shirt are so <u>fiddly</u>, I can barely button them.
E-2: This model airplane is quite <u>fiddly</u> to assemble, requiring patience and precision.

J-1: このシャツの小さいボタンはとても<u>小さくて取り扱いにくい</u>ので、ほとんど留めることができません。
