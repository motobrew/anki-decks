# Key:two sides of the same coin
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Working hard and taking breaks are <u>two sides of the same coin</u> when it comes to productivity.
E-2: Success and failure are often considered <u>two sides of the same coin</u> in the business world.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 生産性に関しては、努力と休憩は同じ<u>メダルの裏表</u>です。
J-2: ビジネス界では、成功と失敗は<u>同じコインの両面</u>と考えられることが多いです。
