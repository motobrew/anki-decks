# Key:long shot!
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: It's a <u>long shot</u>, but I'll try asking my boss for a raise.
E-2: I know it's a <u>long shot</u>, but maybe we can still make it to the movie in time.
E-3: He's not the best candidate, but he's worth a <u>long shot</u> in the upcoming election.
