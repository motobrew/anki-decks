# Key:strip A of B
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The storm <u>stripped the trees of</u> their leaves.
E-2: The company <u>stripped the employee of</u> their benefits after they were caught stealing.
E-3: The government <u>stripped the organization of</u> its non-profit status due to fraudulent activity.
