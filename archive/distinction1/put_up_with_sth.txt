# Key:put up with sth
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I can't <u>put up with</u> the noise from my neighbor's party anymore.
E-2: She had to <u>put up with</u> her boss's unreasonable demands to keep her job.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: もう隣人のパーティからの騒音には<u>我慢できない</u>。
J-2: 彼女は仕事を続けるために上司の不合理な要求に<u>耐えなければならなかった</u>。
