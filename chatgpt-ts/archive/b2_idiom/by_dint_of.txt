# Key:by dint of
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: <u>By dint of</u> hard work and dedication, she was able to achieve her goals.
E-2: The team won the championship <u>by dint of</u> their superior teamwork and strategy.
E-3: <u>By dint of</u> his persuasive arguments, he was able to convince the board to approve the new project.
