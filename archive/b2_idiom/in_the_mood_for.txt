# Key:in the mood for
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I'm <u>in the mood for</u> some ice cream, let's go get some.
E-2: She's not <u>in the mood for</u> socializing tonight, so she's staying in.
E-3: He's always <u>in the mood for</u> a good cup of coffee in the morning.

J-1: アイスクリームが食べたい気分なんだ、一緒に買いに行こう。
