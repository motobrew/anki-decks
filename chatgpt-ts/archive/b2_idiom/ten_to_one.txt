# Key:ten to one
## Response

E-1: It's <u>ten to one</u> that it will rain today so bring an umbrella.
E-2: I'd say it's <u>ten to one</u> that he will show up late for the meeting.
E-3: <u>Ten to one</u> there will be a traffic jam during rush hour.
