# Key:play it by ear
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Let's just <u>play it by ear</u> and decide on the restaurant when we get there.
E-2: I don't have a plan yet, I'll just have to <u>play it by ear</u> for the weekend.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: そこへ着いてからレストランを決めることにしましょう。
J-2: まだ計画がないので、週末は臨機応変にします。
