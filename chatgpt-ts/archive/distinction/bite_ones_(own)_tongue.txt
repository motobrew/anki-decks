# Key:bite one's (own) tongue
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I had to <u>bite my tongue</u> when they started talking about politics at the dinner table.
E-2: She wanted to say something rude, but decided to <u>bite her tongue</u> instead.
E-3: He had to <u>bite his own tongue</u> to keep from laughing at his friend's terrible joke.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 夕食時に政治の話題が出始めた時、私は<u>口を噛んで黙っていました</u>。
J-2: 彼女は失礼なことを言いたかったが、代わりに<u>黙っていることにした</u>。
J-3: 彼は友達のひどいジョークに笑わずにいるために、<u>自分の舌を噛んだ</u>。
