# Key:take the sting out of sth
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Putting some ointment on the burn will <u>take the sting out of it</u>.
E-2: The apology letter <u>took the sting out of</u> the previous argument.

J-1: 焼けた場所に軟膏を塗れば、痛みが<u>和らぎます</u>。
J-2: 謝罪の手紙は、前の口論の<u>辛さを取り除いてくれました</u>。
