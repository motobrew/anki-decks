# Key:stars are aligned
## Response

E-1: I've been working so hard, and finally, the <u>stars are aligned</u> for me to get a promotion.
E-2: This project is finally making progress, it feels like the <u>stars are aligned</u>.
E-3: It's not every day that everything goes smoothly, but today the <u>stars are aligned</u>.
