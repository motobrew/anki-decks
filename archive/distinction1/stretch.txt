# Key:stretch
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I know we're on a tight deadline, but let's not <u>stretch</u> ourselves too thin.
E-2: His story is so unbelievable, he really <u>stretched</u> the truth.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 締め切りが厳しいことはわかっているけど、自分たちを無理しないようにしよう。
J-2: 彼の話は信じがたいほどで、本当のことを<u>大げさに言った</u>。
