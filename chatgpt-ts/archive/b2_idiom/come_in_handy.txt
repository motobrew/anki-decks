# Key:come in handy
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: A Swiss Army Knife can <u>come in handy</u> when you're camping.
E-2: Keeping a first-aid kit in your car can <u>come in handy</u> in case of an emergency.
E-3: Knowing how to speak Spanish can <u>come in handy</u> when traveling in South America.
