## Response

E-1: Can you please <u>watch over</u> my dog while I'm out of town?
E-2: The lifeguard will <u>watch over</u> the pool to make sure everyone is safe.
E-3: You need to <u>watch over</u> the frying pan so that the food doesn't burn.
