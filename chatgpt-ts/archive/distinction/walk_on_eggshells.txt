# Key:walk on eggshells
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I have to <u>walk on eggshells</u> around my boss because she gets angry very easily.
E-2: Whenever my parents are arguing, I feel like I have to <u>walk on eggshells</u> around them.
E-3: The new employee was nervous and <u>walking on eggshells</u> during his first day at work.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 目が怒りっぽいので、上司の周りでは<u>気を遣う</u>必要があります。
J-2: 両親が口論しているときは、私は彼らの周りでいつも<u>慎重に行動</u>しなければなりません。
J-3: 新しい社員は緊張して、初めての出勤日に<u>慎重に行動</u>していました。
