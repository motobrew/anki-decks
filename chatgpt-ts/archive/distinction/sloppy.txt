# Key:sloppy
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I can't believe you spilled your drink all over the table, you're so <u>sloppy</u>!
E-2: His handwriting is so <u>sloppy</u>, I can't even read what he wrote.
E-3: She didn't clean the kitchen after cooking, it's so <u>sloppy</u>.
