# Key:go astray
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Sometimes, well-intentioned people can <u>go astray</u> and end up causing harm instead of help.
E-2: The hiker <u>went astray</u> from the trail and got lost in the woods.
E-3: The company's profits <u>went astray</u> due to mismanagement and corruption.
