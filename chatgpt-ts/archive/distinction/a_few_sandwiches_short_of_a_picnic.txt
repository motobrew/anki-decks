# Key:a few sandwiches short of a picnic
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I tried to have a conversation with him, but it was clear that he was 'a few sandwiches short of a picnic'.
E-2: She spent all her money on useless items; some might say she's 'a few sandwiches short of a picnic'.
E-3: His behavior was so strange, it was like he was 'a few sandwiches short of a picnic'.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼と会話をしようとしたけど、彼が何を言ってるか全然理解できなくて、「彼バカ？」と思った。
J-2: 彼女が役に立たないものにお金を使い切ったから、「彼女馬鹿？」と言われるかもしれない。
J-3: 彼の行動がとても奇妙だった。彼はまるで「まともな状態でない」とでも言いたげだった。
