# Key:run off
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The water in the kettle has boiled, I'm going to <u>pour it off</u> for my tea.
E-2: I accidentally knocked over the vase and the flowers <u>ran off</u> onto the table.
E-3: The children were misbehaving so the teacher had to <u>run off</u> copies of the classroom rules.
