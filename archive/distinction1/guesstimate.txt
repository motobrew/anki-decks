# Key:guesstimate
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I can't give you an exact cost, but I can <u>guesstimate</u> it will be around $100.
E-2: I'll <u>guesstimate</u> that we'll arrive at the airport around 6 pm.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 正確な費用をお伝えできませんが、およそ100ドルぐらいの<u>概算見積もり</u>になります。
J-2: 空港に着くのはおよそ午後6時と<u>推測されます</u>。
