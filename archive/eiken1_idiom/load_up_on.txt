# Key:load up on
<h3>English Sentences</h3>
E-1: Before going camping, we need to <u>load up on</u> food and supplies.
E-2: The team decided to <u>load up on</u> experienced players for the upcoming tournament.

J-1: キャンプに行く前に、食料品と用具を<u>たくさん買い込まなければならない</u>。
