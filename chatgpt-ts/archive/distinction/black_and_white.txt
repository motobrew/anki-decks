# Key:black and white
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The instructions for assembling the furniture are clear and <u>black and white</u>.
E-2: The contract states the terms of the agreement in <u>black and white</u>.
E-3: Life is not <u>black and white</u>, there are many grey areas.
