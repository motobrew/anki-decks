# Key:elaborate on
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Can you please <u>elaborate on</u> your idea? I don't quite understand.
E-2: The professor asked me to <u>elaborate on</u> my research findings during the presentation.
E-3: The witness was asked to <u>elaborate on</u> what he saw during the crime.
