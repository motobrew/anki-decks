# Key:assiduous
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: She was assiduous in her studies, spending countless hours pouring over books and notes.
E-2: His assiduous attention to detail was evident in the impeccable work he produced.
E-3: The company's success can be attributed to the assiduous efforts of its hardworking employees.

J-1: 彼女は勉強に熱心で、何時間も本やノートを見て過ごしました。

J-3: 企業の成功は、一生懸命働く従業員たちの努力によるものと言えます。
