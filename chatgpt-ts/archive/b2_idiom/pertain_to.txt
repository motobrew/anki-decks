# Key:pertain to
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The lecture will <u>pertain to</u> the history of ancient Rome.
E-2: The new regulations <u>pertain to</u> safety in the workplace.
E-3: This book will <u>pertain to</u> the subject of biology.
