# Key:check sth out
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: You should <u>check out</u> the new restaurant in town, the food is amazing.
E-2: I'm going to <u>check out</u> that new movie that just came out.
E-3: Have you had a chance to <u>check out</u> the latest album from your favorite artist?

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 街に新しくできたレストランに行ってみたら？料理がすごく美味しいよ。
J-2: この前公開された新しい映画を<u>見に行く</u>つもりだ。
J-3: 好きなアーティストの最新アルバムを<u>チェック</u>できた？
