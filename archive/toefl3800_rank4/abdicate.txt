# Key:abdicate
### English Sentences
E-1: When the king grew old, he decided to <u>abdicate</u> the throne.
E-2: The CEO of the company decided to <u>abdicate</u> his responsibilities and retire.

J-1: 王様が年をとると、王位を<u>退位する</u>ことに決めました。
