# Key:bear out
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The evidence found at the crime scene <u>bears out</u> the suspect's alibi.
E-2: The results of the experiment <u>bear out</u> the hypothesis.
E-3: Her story was <u>bear out</u> by several witnesses.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 犯罪現場で見つかった証拠は容疑者のアリバイを<u>裏付ける</u>。
J-2: 実験の結果は仮説を<u>支持する</u>。
J-3: 彼女の話は複数の証言で<u>証明される</u>。
