# Key:infringe on
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The new copyright law could <u>infringe on</u> the rights of content creators.
E-2: The company's new policy may <u>infringe on</u> employees' privacy.
E-3: We have to be careful not to <u>infringe on</u> the intellectual property of others.

J-3: 私たちは他人の知的財産を<u>侵害しないように</u>注意しなければなりません。
