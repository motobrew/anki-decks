# Key:settle one's differences
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The two friends finally decided to <u>settle their differences</u> and make up.
E-2: The company's CEO had a meeting with the rival CEO to <u>settle their differences</u> and reach a compromise.
E-3: The politicians held talks to <u>settle their differences</u> and avoid a potential war.
