# Key:be faithful to
## Response

E-1: You should always <u>be faithful to</u> your partner in a relationship.
E-2: As a soldier, you must <u>be faithful to</u> your country and protect it from harm.
E-3: Fans are expected to <u>be faithful to</u> their favorite team through wins and losses.
