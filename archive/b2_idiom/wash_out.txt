# Key:wash out
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The rain was so heavy that it <u>washed out</u> the game.
E-2: The flood <u>washed out</u> the bridge and now we can't cross the river.
E-3: I forgot to put sunscreen on and now the sweat is making it <u>wash out</u> of my skin.
