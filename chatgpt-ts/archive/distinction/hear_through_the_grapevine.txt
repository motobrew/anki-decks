# Key:hear through the grapevine
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I heard through the grapevine that Sarah got promoted to manager.
E-2: Have you heard through the grapevine that Jake and Emily broke up?
E-3: My friends heard through the grapevine that there's going to be a surprise party for me.
