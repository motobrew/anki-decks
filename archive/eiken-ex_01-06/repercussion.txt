# Key:repercussion
### English Sentences
E-1: The reckless action had serious <u>repercussions</u> on his career.
E-2: The company's decision to downsize has had negative <u>repercussions</u> on the economy.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: その無謀な行動は彼のキャリアに重大な<u>影響</u>を与えました。
J-2: 会社の縮小の決定は経済に悪い<u>影響</u>を与えました。
