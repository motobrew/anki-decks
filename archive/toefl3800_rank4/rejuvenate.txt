# Key:rejuvenate
### English Sentences
E-1: I like to go on vacations to <u>rejuvenate</u> and relax by the beach.
E-2: Listening to music can <u>rejuvenate</u> my mood after a long day.

J-1: 私は休暇に行って海でくつろいで<u>元気を取り戻す</u>ことが好きです。
