# Key:panacea
<h3>English Sentences</h3>
E-1: A <u>panacea</u> does not exist for all of life's problems.
E-2: She hoped that the new medication would be a <u>panacea</u> for her illness.

J-1: 人生のすべての問題のための<u>万能薬</u>は存在しません。
