# Key:pull together
## Response

E-1: In order to succeed, we need to <u>pull together</u> and work as a team.
E-2: The community was able to <u>pull together</u> and raise enough funds for the new playground.
E-3: During a crisis, it's important for everyone to <u>pull together</u> and support each other.
