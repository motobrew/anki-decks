# Key:educated guess
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I don't have all the information yet, but I can make an <u>educated guess</u> about what happened.
E-2: Based on my <u>educated guess</u>, the restaurant is probably closed on Mondays.
E-3: It's just an <u>educated guess</u>, but I think the company will announce layoffs soon.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: まだすべての情報が揃っていませんが、私は何が起こったか<u>推測</u>できます。
J-2: 私の<u>推測</u>によれば、そのレストランは月曜日はお休みのようです。
J-3: 単なる<u>推測</u>ですが、この会社はすぐにレイオフを発表すると思います。
