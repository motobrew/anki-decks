# Key:dwindle
### English Sentences
E-1: The population of endangered species continues to <u>dwindle</u> each year.
E-2: Our savings account has been slowly <u>dwindling</u> due to unexpected expenses.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 絶滅危惧種の個体数は毎年<u>減少し続けています</u>。
J-2: 思いもよらない出費のため、当座預金が徐々に<u>減っていく</u>。
