# Key:engrossed
### English Sentences
E-1: She was <u>engrossed</u> in the book and couldn't put it down.
E-2: The children were <u>engrossed</u> in their video game and didn't notice their parents calling them.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼女はその本に夢中で、手放すことができませんでした。
J-2: 子供たちはビデオゲームに夢中で、親が呼んでいるのに気づかなかった。
