# Key:leave out
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Don't <u>leave out</u> any important details when you're writing the report.
E-2: I accidentally <u>left out</u> your name from the invitation list, I'm sorry.
E-3: Make sure to <u>leave out</u> the mushrooms from the salad, I'm allergic to them.
