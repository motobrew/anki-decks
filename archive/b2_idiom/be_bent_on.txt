# Key:be bent on
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: She is <u>bent on</u> becoming a doctor and is currently studying pre-med.
E-2: He is <u>bent on</u> winning the competition and has been practicing for months.
E-3: The company is <u>bent on</u> expanding into international markets.
