# Key:split the bill
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Let's <u>split the bill</u> evenly between everyone at the table.
E-2: We always <u>split the bill</u> when we go out to eat with friends.
E-3: Can we please <u>split the bill</u> so that I can pay for my own meal?
