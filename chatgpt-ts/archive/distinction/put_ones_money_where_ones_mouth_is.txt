# Key:put one's money where one's mouth is
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: If you believe in this project so much, why don't you <u>put your money where your mouth is</u> and invest in it?
E-2: She keeps talking about how she's going to start working out, but she never does. She needs to <u>put her money where her mouth is</u>.
E-3: If you're going to talk about how important education is, you need to <u>put your money where your mouth is</u> and donate to schools.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: このプロジェクトをそんなに信じてるなら、自分のお金を出して投資することに<u>言葉責めするなら、行動に移しろ</u>。
J-2: 彼女は運動を始めるつもりだと話すけど、実際はやらない。行動に移すべきだ<u>言葉責めするなら、行動に移しろ</u>。
J-3: 教育の重要性を口で語るなら、学校に寄付する<u>言葉責めするなら、行動に移しろ</u>。
