# Key:build A into B
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: They plan to <u>build a new facility</u> into the existing warehouse space.
E-2: The company wants to <u>build a strong brand</u> into the market with its new product line.
E-3: The entrepreneur is trying to <u>build a community</u> into their online platform.

J-1: 彼らは既存の倉庫スペースに新しい施設を<u>建設する</u>予定です。

J-3: 起業家はオンラインプラットフォームにコミュニティを<u>構築する</u>ように努めています。
