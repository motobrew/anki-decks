# Key:drag sb's name through the mud
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: She spread lies about her former colleague, <u>dragging her name through the mud</u>.
E-2: Don't <u>drag my name through the mud</u> just because you're upset with me.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼女は以前の同僚について嘘を流し、<u>彼女の名誉を傷つけた</u>。
J-2: 怒っているからといって、<u>私の名前を貶めないで</u>。
