# Key:obstinate
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The cat was so <u>obstinate</u> that it refused to come down from the tree.
E-2: Even after receiving a bad grade, the student remained <u>obstinate</u> about their study habits.
E-3: The politician was known for their <u>obstinate</u> refusal to compromise on any issue.
