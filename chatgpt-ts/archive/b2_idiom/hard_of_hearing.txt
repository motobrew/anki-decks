# Key:hard of hearing
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: My grandfather is <u>hard of hearing</u> and often has to ask people to repeat what they say.
E-2: The conference room is equipped with microphones and speakers to accommodate attendees who are <u>hard of hearing</u>.
E-3: The theater offers special devices for patrons who are <u>hard of hearing</u> to enhance their viewing experience.
