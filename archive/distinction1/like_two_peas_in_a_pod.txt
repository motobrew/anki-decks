# Key:like two peas in a pod
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Sarah and Emma are <u>like two peas in a pod</u>, they do everything together.
E-2: The twins look nothing alike, but they are <u>like two peas in a pod</u> when it comes to personalities.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: サラとエマは<u>似たようなもの同士</u>で、いつも一緒にいます。
J-2: 双子は見かけが全く違いますが、性格は<u>瓜二つ</u>です。
