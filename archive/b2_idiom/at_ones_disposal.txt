# Key:at one's disposal
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: With all the resources now <u>at my disposal</u>, I think I can finally finish this project.
E-2: The company provided the team with all the tools <u>at their disposal</u> to succeed.
E-3: She was a great CEO who had a vast amount of knowledge and experience <u>at her disposal</u>.
