# Key:surmount
## Response

E-1: With hard work and determination, you can <u>surmount</u> any obstacle in your way.
E-2: The team was able to <u>surmount</u> their previous losses and win the championship game.
E-3: She felt a great sense of accomplishment when she was finally able to <u>surmount</u> her fear of public speaking.
