# Key:plagiarism
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The student was accused of <u>plagiarism</u> for copying entire paragraphs from an online article.
E-2: The consequences of <u>plagiarism</u> in academic writing can include a failing grade or even expulsion.
E-3: To avoid <u>plagiarism</u>, it is important to properly cite all sources used in a research paper.
