# Key:to some degree
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: To some degree, I agree with your opinion on the matter.
E-2: The project was successful to some degree, but there were still some issues.
E-3: Learning a new language is difficult, but to some degree, it can be rewarding.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: その件について、ある程度あなたの意見に賛成です。
J-2: プロジェクトはある程度成功したが、まだ問題があった。
J-3: 新しい言語を学ぶ事は難しいが、ある程度報われるかもしれない。
