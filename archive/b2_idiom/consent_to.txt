# Key:consent to
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: She didn't <u>consent to</u> the medical procedure until the doctor explained it more thoroughly.
E-2: The contract explicitly states that both parties must <u>consent to</u> any changes.
E-3: Without <u>consent from</u> the owner, the construction company cannot start building on the property.
