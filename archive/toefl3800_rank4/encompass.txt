# Key:encompass
### English Sentences
E-1: The museum's exhibits <u>encompass</u> a wide range of historical artifacts.
E-2: The new legislation will <u>encompass</u> all aspects of environmental protection.

J-1: その博物館の展示品は、幅広い歴史的な遺物を<u>包括しています</u>。
