# Key:double-check sth/(that)
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Before you submit your essay, make sure to <u>double-check</u> your citations and references.
E-2: I always <u>double-check</u> my schedule for the day before going to bed.
E-3: The accountant will <u>double-check</u> the tax documents before submitting them to the government.
