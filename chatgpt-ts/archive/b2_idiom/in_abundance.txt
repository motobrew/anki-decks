# Key:in abundance
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: During autumn, fresh fruit is available <u>in abundance</u> at the farmer's market.
E-2: The ocean has a variety of fish and sea creatures <u>in abundance</u>.
E-3: The tropical rainforest is home to many exotic plants and animals <u>in abundance</u>.

J-3: 熱帯雨林には、多くのエキゾチックな植物や動物が<u>豊富に</u>生息しています。
