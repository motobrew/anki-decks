# Key:take it easy
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I know the exam is tomorrow, but <u>take it easy</u> and get some sleep.
E-2: Don't worry about finishing the project today, <u>take it easy</u> and pace yourself.

J-1: 試験は明日だけど、<u>ゆっくり</u>寝てね。
J-2: 今日プロジェクトを終わらせることに<u>焦らずゆっくりと</u>取り組んでね。
