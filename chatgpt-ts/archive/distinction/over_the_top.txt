# Key:over the top
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: That party last night was so <u>over the top</u>. They had a live band, a DJ, and a catered buffet.
E-2: I appreciate your enthusiasm, but let's not go <u>over the top</u> with the decorations for the office Christmas party.
E-3: The coach's reaction to the loss was way <u>over the top</u>. He was screaming and throwing things in the locker room.
