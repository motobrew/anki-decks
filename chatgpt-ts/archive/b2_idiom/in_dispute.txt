# Key:in dispute
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The ownership of the land is <u>in dispute</u> between the two families.
E-2: The company's decision is currently <u>in dispute</u> among the board members.
E-3: The validity of the contract is <u>in dispute</u> due to unclear terms.
