# Key:on the dot
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Make sure you arrive at the meeting <u>on the dot</u> at 9 AM.
E-2: The train always arrives <u>on the dot</u> at this station.
E-3: I have to leave <u>on the dot</u> because I have another appointment right after this.
