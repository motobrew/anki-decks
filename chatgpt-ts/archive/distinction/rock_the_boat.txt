# Key:rock the boat
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I don't want to <u>rock the boat</u>, but I think your idea might not work.
E-2: He didn't want to <u>rock the boat</u> and kept quiet even though he disagreed.
E-3: The new employee didn't want to <u>rock the boat</u> on the first day, so he followed everything his boss said.

J-3: 新しい社員は初日には揉めたくないと考え、上司の言う通りに全て従った。
