# Key:straight and narrow
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He's been trying to stay on the <u>straight and narrow</u> ever since he got out of rehab.
E-2: My parents always told me to stay on the <u>straight and narrow</u> and not get into trouble.
E-3: After getting into trouble with the law, he decided to turn his life around and stay on the <u>straight and narrow</u>.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼はリハビリから出て以来、<u>正しい道を歩む</u>ように努めています。
J-2: 親は常に私に<u>真っ当で正しい道を歩む</u>ように語りかけてくれました。
J-3: 法律に違反した後、彼は人生を変えて<u>正しい道を歩む</u>ことに決めました。
