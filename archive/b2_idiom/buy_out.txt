# Key:buy out
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The company decided to <u>buy out</u> its competitor to increase its market share.
E-2: We offered to <u>buy out</u> our partner's shares to take full control of the business.
E-3: The wealthy investor was able to <u>buy out</u> the struggling startup and turn it into a profitable company.
