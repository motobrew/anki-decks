# Key:in one's presence
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: It's important to behave respectfully <u>in one's presence</u> to make a good impression.
E-2: He always tries to be on his best behavior <u>in his boss's presence</u>.
E-3: The performer felt nervous <u>in the presence</u> of the famous celebrity.

J-2: 彼はいつも、<u>上司の前で</u>優れた振る舞いをしようと努力しています。
