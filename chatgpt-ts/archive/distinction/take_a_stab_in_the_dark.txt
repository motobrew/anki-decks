# Key:take a stab in the dark
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I'm not sure what the answer is, but let me <u>take a stab in the dark</u>.
E-2: I'll <u>take a stab in the dark</u> and say that she's probably not coming to the party.
E-3: We have no idea where the restaurant is, but we can <u>take a stab in the dark</u> and hope we find it.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 答えがわからないけど、<u>推測してみる</u>ね。
J-2: 彼女はパーティーに来ないと<u>予測してみる</u>よ。
J-3: レストランがどこかわからないけど、<u>適当に当てずっぽうで探してみる</u>しかない。
