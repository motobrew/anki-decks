# Key:stumbling block
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The lack of experience in web development can be a <u>stumbling block</u> for those trying to create their own website.
E-2: His fear of failure became a <u>stumbling block</u> to pursuing his dreams.
E-3: The language barrier can be a <u>stumbling block</u> to effective communication in a multicultural workplace.
