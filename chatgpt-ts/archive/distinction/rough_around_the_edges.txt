# Key:rough around the edges
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: His manners are a bit <u>rough around the edges</u>, but he means well.
E-2: The old car may be <u>rough around the edges</u>, but it still runs great.
E-3: She's a great singer, but her performance was a little <u>rough around the edges</u> tonight.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼のマナーは少し荒々しいですが、よい意味を持っています。
J-2: 古い車は少し手入れが行き届いていませんが、まだ素晴らしく走っています。
J-3: 彼女は優れた歌手ですが、今夜のパフォーマンスは少し上手くいかなかった。
