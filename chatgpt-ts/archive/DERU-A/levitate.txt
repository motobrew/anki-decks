# Key:levitate
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The magician was able to <u>levitate</u> off the ground and float in mid-air.
E-2: Some people claim that they can <u>levitate</u> objects with their mind.
E-3: In the movie, the character was able to <u>levitate</u> objects by using a special power.
