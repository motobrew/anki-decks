# Key:put through
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Can you please <u>put me through</u> to the manager's office?
E-2: The receptionist will <u>put you through</u> to the customer service department.
E-3: I tried calling my friend, but I couldn't <u>put through</u> the call.
