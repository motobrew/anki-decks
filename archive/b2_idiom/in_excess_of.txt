# Key:in excess of
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The company's revenue was <u>in excess of</u> $1 million last year.
E-2: The marathon runner ran <u>in excess of</u> 26 miles during the race.
E-3: The weight limit for the elevator was <u>in excess of</u> 800 pounds.
