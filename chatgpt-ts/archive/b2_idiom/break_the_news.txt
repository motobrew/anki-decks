# Key:break the news
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I have to <u>break the news</u> to my parents that I'm dropping out of college.
E-2: The doctor will <u>break the news</u> to the patient that they have a serious illness.
E-3: How should I <u>break the news</u> to my boss that I'm quitting my job?
