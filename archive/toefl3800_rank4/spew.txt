# Key:spew
### English Sentences
E-1: The volcano began to <u>spew</u> hot lava and ash into the sky.
E-2: The politician's speech was filled with hateful rhetoric, constantly <u>spewing</u> divisive language.

J-1: 火山が熱い溶岩と灰を空に<u>噴出し始めました</u>。
