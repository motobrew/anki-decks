# Key:foster
### English Sentences
E-1: She has dedicated her life to <u>foster</u> the arts and culture in her community.
E-2: The company aims to <u>foster</u> a collaborative work environment by encouraging teamwork.

J-1: 彼女は自分のコミュニティで芸術と文化を<u>育てるために</u>一生懸命努力しています。
