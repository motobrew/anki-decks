# Key:take pains
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He <u>took pains</u> to make sure the report was accurate and complete.
E-2: She <u>takes pains</u> to keep her yard immaculate and beautiful.
E-3: The chef <u>took pains</u> to prepare the meal exactly to the customer's specifications.
