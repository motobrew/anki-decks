# Key:come away
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Let's <u>come away</u> from the busy city and spend some time in nature.
E-2: When the argument got heated, she needed to <u>come away</u> to cool down.
E-3: It's hard for me to <u>come away</u> from my work and take a break.

J-3: 仕事から<u>離れて</u>休憩をとることが私には難しいです。
