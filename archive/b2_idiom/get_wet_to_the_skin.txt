# Key:get wet to the skin
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I forgot to bring an umbrella and ended up <u>getting wet to the skin</u> in the rain.
E-2: The kids didn't listen and went swimming in the lake, <u>getting wet to the skin</u> despite the chilly weather.
E-3: The athlete dove into the pool, <u>getting wet to the skin</u> as she started training.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 傘を持ってこず、雨で<u>びしょ濡れになってしまった</u>。
J-2: 子供たちは聞かずに湖で泳ぎ、寒い天気の中でも<u>ずぶ濡れになってしまった</u>。
J-3: 選手はプールに飛び込み、トレーニングを始めた際、<u>肌までびしょ濡れになった</u>。
