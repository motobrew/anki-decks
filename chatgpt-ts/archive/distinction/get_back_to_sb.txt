# Key:get back to sb
## Response

E-1: Let me <u>get back to you</u> on that proposal after I review it.
E-2: Sorry, I can't talk right now. I'll <u>get back to you</u> later.
E-3: She said she would <u>get back to me</u> with an answer by the end of the day.
