# Key:jack-of-all-trades
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He can fix the car, paint the house, and cook a meal, he's a <u>jack-of-all-trades</u>.
E-2: Although he didn't have any specific skills, he became the company's go-to <u>jack-of-all-trades</u>.
