# Key:look on the bright side
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Even though I lost my job, I can <u>look on the bright side</u> and finally start my own business.
E-2: My car broke down, but <u>look on the bright side</u>, at least I can get some exercise by walking to work.
E-3: It's raining on our vacation, but <u>look on the bright side</u>, we can still have fun playing board games indoors.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 仕事を失ったけど、<u>前向きに考えて</u>自分のビジネスを始められるようになるかもしれない。
J-2: 車が故障したけど、<u>明るい面を見ると</u>、歩いて仕事に行って運動できるしね。
J-3: 旅行中に雨が降ってきたけど、<u>前向きに考えれば</u>、屋内でボードゲームをして楽しめるよ。
