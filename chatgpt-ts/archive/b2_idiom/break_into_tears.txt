# Key:break into tears
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: When she heard the news, she couldn't help but <u>break into tears</u>.
E-2: The child was so frightened by the loud noise that he <u>broke into tears</u>.
E-3: Seeing his longtime friend at the airport after years apart, he <u>broke into tears</u>.
