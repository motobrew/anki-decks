# Key:walk off with
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I can't find my phone. Someone must have <u>walked off with</u> it.
E-2: The party was a mess. People were <u>walking off with</u> drinks and food left and right.
E-3: The store owner caught a shoplifter trying to <u>walk off with</u> a candy bar.
