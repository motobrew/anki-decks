# Key:stick out like a sore thumb
## Response

E-1: His neon green shirt <u>sticks out like a sore thumb</u> in a sea of black suits.
E-2: The new modern house <u>sticks out like a sore thumb</u> in the historic neighborhood.
E-3: Her terrible dance moves <u>stick out like a sore thumb</u> in the graceful ballet performance.
