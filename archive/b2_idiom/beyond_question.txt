# Key:beyond question
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: It is <u>beyond question</u> that the Earth orbits around the Sun.
E-2: His expertise in the field is <u>beyond question</u>.
E-3: The fact that smoking causes cancer is <u>beyond question</u>.
