# Key:hand down
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: My grandfather <u>handed down</u> his favorite watch to me before he passed away.
E-2: The tradition of making homemade pasta has been <u>handed down</u> in my family for generations.
E-3: The company's CEO plans to <u>hand down</u> his decision to the employees next week.
