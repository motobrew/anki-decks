# Key:drag one's feet
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Stop <u>dragging your feet</u> and finish your homework already!
E-2: We've been <u>dragging our feet</u> on making a decision about the new office location.
E-3: The company is <u>dragging its feet</u> on implementing the new policy.
