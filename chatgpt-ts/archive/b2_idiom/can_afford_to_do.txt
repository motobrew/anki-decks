# Key:can afford to do
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: She <u>can afford to buy</u> a new car with her savings.
E-2: They <u>can't afford to take</u> a vacation this year due to financial difficulties.
E-3: He <u>can afford to quit</u> his job because he has another offer.
