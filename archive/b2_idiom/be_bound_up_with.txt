# Key:be bound up with
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The success of the project will <u>be bound up with</u> the collaboration of all team members.
E-2: The country's history <u>is bound up with</u> its culture, traditions, and beliefs.
E-3: Her happiness <u>is bound up with</u> the well-being of her family.
