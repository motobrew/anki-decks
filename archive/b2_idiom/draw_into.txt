# Key:draw into
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The artist used charcoal to <u>draw into</u> the paper and create a detailed portrait.
E-2: The teacher asked the students to <u>draw into</u> their prior knowledge to help them solve the math problem.
E-3: The company decided to <u>draw into</u> its reserves to pay for the new project.
