# Key:lay down
## Response

E-1: I'm feeling really tired, I think I'm going to <u>lay down</u> for a little while.
E-2: My cat likes to <u>lay down</u> in the sunbeam coming through the window.
E-3: Before we start our yoga practice, let's all <u>lay down</u> on our mats and take a few deep breaths.
