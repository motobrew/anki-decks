# Key:misgiving
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: She had a <u>misgiving</u> about accepting the job offer because of the long commute.
E-2: He couldn't shake off the <u>misgiving</u> that something was off about the company's financial statements.
E-3: The team had a <u>misgiving</u> about their new manager's lack of experience in the industry.
