# Key:be indebted to
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I will forever <u>be indebted to</u> my parents for their unwavering support.
E-2: She feels that she will <u>be indebted to</u> her mentor for life-changing advice.
E-3: The whole community is <u>be indebted to</u> the volunteer firefighters who saved their homes from the wildfire.
