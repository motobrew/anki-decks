# Key:keep sb up at night
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The thought of the upcoming exam is really <u>keeping me up at night</u>.
E-2: The noise from the party next door <u>kept me up all night</u>.
E-3: The fear of losing my job <u>keeps me up at night</u>.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: もうすぐの試験のことが本当に<u>心配で夜も眠れない</u>。
J-2: 隣のパーティーからの騒音で一晩中<u>寝られなかった</u>。
J-3: 仕事を失うことへの不安が、私を夜更かし<u>させる</u>。
