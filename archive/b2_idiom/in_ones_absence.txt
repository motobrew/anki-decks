# Key:in one's absence
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: In one's absence, the assistant manager will take charge of the store.
E-2: The CEO left strict instructions to follow in his absence.
E-3: It's important to have a backup plan in one's absence.

J-2: CEOは自身の不在中に従うべき厳しい指示を残しました。
J-3: 不在時に備えてバックアッププランを立てることが重要です。
