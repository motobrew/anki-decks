# Key:on the fly
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I had to come up with a speech <u>on the fly</u> when the keynote speaker didn't show up.
E-2: The chef improvised a new dish <u>on the fly</u> to accommodate the special request.

J-1: 基調講演者が現れないため、私は即興でスピーチを<u>作らなければならなかった</u>。
