# Key:walk away with
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: After the job interview, I was happy to <u>walk away with</u> a job offer.
E-2: The lucky winner will <u>walk away with</u> a brand new car after the raffle.
E-3: Despite the heavy competition, our team was able to <u>walk away with</u> first place in the tournament.
