# Key:carve up
<h3>English Sentences</h3>
E-1: The chef skillfully <u>carves up</u> the roasted turkey for Thanksgiving dinner.
E-2: The siblings always <u>carve up</u> the pizza equally to make sure everyone gets a fair share.
