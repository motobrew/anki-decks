# Key:behind sb's back
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Don't talk about me <u>behind my back</u>.
E-2: I can't trust her, she always talks <u>behind people's backs</u>.
E-3: It's not fair to criticize someone <u>behind their back</u>.
