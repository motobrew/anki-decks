# Key:gratify
### English Sentences
E-1: She bought a new dress to <u>gratify</u> herself after a long week at work.
E-2: The company gave him a bonus to <u>gratify</u> him for his hard work.

J-1: 彼女は働き詰めの一週間の後、自分自身を<u>喜ばせるために</u>新しいドレスを買いました。
