# Key:quite a bit
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I've been going to the gym <u>quite a bit</u> lately, trying to improve my overall fitness.
E-2: My boss has been traveling <u>quite a bit</u> for work, leaving me in charge of the office.
E-3: We saved <u>quite a bit</u> of money by cooking at home instead of eating out every night.
