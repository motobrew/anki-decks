# Key:abound in
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The forest <u>abounds in</u> diverse wildlife and plant species.
E-2: The market <u>abounds in</u> fresh produce and artisanal goods.
E-3: This book <u>abounds in</u> useful tips and tricks for home improvement.
