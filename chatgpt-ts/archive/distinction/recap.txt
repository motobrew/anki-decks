# Key:recap
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Let me <u>recap</u> what we discussed in the meeting.
E-2: Can you <u>recap</u> the key points of the presentation for me?
E-3: I missed the first episode of the show, can you <u>recap</u> it for me?
