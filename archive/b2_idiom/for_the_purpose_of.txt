# Key:for the purpose of
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: <u>For the purpose of</u> keeping the classroom clean, students must sweep the floors after class.
E-2: The company will conduct a background check <u>for the purpose of</u> verifying the applicant's employment history.
E-3: The new law was enacted <u>for the purpose of</u> reducing carbon emissions.
