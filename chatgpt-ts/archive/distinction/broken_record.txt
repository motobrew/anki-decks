# Key:broken record
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I feel like I'm a <u>broken record</u> always reminding my kids to brush their teeth.
E-2: The teacher kept repeating the same instructions, sounding like a <u>broken record</u>.
E-3: My boss keeps giving me the same lecture every day, it's like a <u>broken record</u>.
