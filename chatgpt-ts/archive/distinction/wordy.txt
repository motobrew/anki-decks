# Key:wordy
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He always talks so <u>wordy</u>, it's hard to understand his point.
E-2: The article was too <u>wordy</u>, I didn't finish reading it.
E-3: Can you make your presentation less <u>wordy</u>? I don't have much time.
