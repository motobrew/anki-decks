# Key:deplorable
### English Sentences
E-1: The condition of the building was <u>deplorable</u> and in need of immediate repairs.
E-2: His behavior towards his colleagues was <u>deplorable</u> and completely unacceptable.

J-1: 建物の状態は <u>ひどく悲惨な</u> 状況で、すぐに修理が必要でした。
