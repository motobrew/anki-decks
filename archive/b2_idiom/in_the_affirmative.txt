# Key:in the affirmative
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: She responded <u>in the affirmative</u> to his proposal.
E-2: The boss nodded <u>in the affirmative</u> to their request for a pay raise.
E-3: When the teacher asked if everyone had finished their homework, all the students answered <u>in the affirmative</u>.
