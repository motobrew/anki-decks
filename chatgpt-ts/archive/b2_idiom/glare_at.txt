# Key:glare at
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: She <u>glared at</u> him when he interrupted her during the meeting.
E-2: The teacher <u>glared at</u> the noisy students to make them quiet down.
E-3: I could feel my boss <u>glaring at</u> me when I came in late to work.
