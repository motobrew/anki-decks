# Key:be applicable to
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The new policy will <u>be applicable to</u> all employees starting next month.
E-2: These safety regulations only <u>be applicable to</u> workers in hazardous environments.
E-3: This discount code will <u>be applicable to</u> your next purchase on our website.
