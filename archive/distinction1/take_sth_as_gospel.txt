# Key:take sth as gospel
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Don't <u>take his word as gospel</u>, he's known to exaggerate.
E-2: Just because it's on the internet doesn't mean you should <u>take it as gospel</u>.

J-2: インターネットに載っているからといって、<u>必ずしも正しいと思わないで</u>。
