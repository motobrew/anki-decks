# Key:take offense at
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I didn't mean to offend you, so please don't <u>take offense at</u> what I said.
E-2: Some people may <u>take offense at</u> jokes that are considered offensive.
E-3: It's important to be mindful of others' feelings and not <u>take offense at</u> things that were not meant to be hurtful.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: あなたを怒らせるつもりではなかったので、私が言ったことに<u>反発しない</u>でください。
J-2: 攻撃的と考えられる冗談に<u>嫌悪感を抱く</u>人もいます。
J-3: 傷つける意図のないことに<u>憤慨しない</u>ように、他人の気持ちに気を配ることが重要です。
