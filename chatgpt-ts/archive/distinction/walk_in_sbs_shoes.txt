# Key:walk in sb's shoes
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: If you could just <u>walk in my shoes</u> for a day, you'd see how difficult my job really is. 
E-2: Before you judge her, try to <u>walk a mile in her shoes</u> and understand what she's going through. 
E-3: It's easy to criticize, but until you <u>walk in someone's shoes</u>, you don't know their struggles.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: ちょっと1日私の靴に<u>身を置いて</u>、私の仕事がどれだけ難しいか分かるはずです。
J-2: 彼女を批判する前に、彼女の靴で1マイル<u>歩いてみて</u>、彼女が何を経験しているか理解しよう。
J-3: 批判するのは簡単ですが、誰かの靴で<u>歩いてみる</u>まで、彼らの苦労はわかりません。
