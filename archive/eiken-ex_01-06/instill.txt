# Key:instill
### English Sentences
E-1: The teacher tried to <u>instill</u> a love of reading in her students.
E-2: Parents often try to <u>instill</u> good values and morals in their children.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 先生は学生たちに読書の愛を<u>植え付けようとしました</u>。
J-2: 親はよく子供たちに良い価値観や道徳を<u>植え付けようとします</u>。
