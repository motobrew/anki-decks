# Key:respite
### English Sentences
E-1: The family took a <u>respite</u> from their busy lives and went on vacation.
E-2: After hours of studying, she took a short <u>respite</u> to clear her mind.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 家族は忙しい生活から<u>一時の休息</u>を取り、休暇に出かけました。
J-2: 数時間の勉強の後、彼女は頭を整理するために<u>短い休憩</u>を取りました。
