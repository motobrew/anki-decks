# Key:prod
### English Sentences
E-1: The salesman used his charm to <u>prod</u> the customer into making a purchase.
E-2: The coach continued to <u>prod</u> the team until they gave their best performance.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: セールスマンは魅力を使い、顧客を買い物に<u>誘い込んだ</u>。
J-2: コーチはチームが最高のパフォーマンスを発揮するまで<u>駆り立て続けた</u>。
