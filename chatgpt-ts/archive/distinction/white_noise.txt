# Key:white noise
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I can't concentrate with all this <u>white noise</u> in the background.
E-2: The politician's speech was just <u>white noise</u> - filled with empty promises and cliches.
E-3: After a long day at work, I like to listen to some <u>white noise</u> to help me fall asleep.
