# Key:pedantic
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: His <u>pedantic</u> way of speaking made it difficult for others to follow his train of thought.
E-2: The professor's <u>pedantic</u> lectures were often seen as tedious by his students.
E-3: She was criticized for being <u>pedantic</u> in her writing style, making her work seem dry and emotionless.
