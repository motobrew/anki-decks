# Key:travesty
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The decision to cut funding to the after-school program was a <u>travesty</u>.
E-2: The company's treatment of its employees during the pandemic was a <u>travesty</u> of justice.
E-3: The film adaptation of the beloved book was a <u>travesty</u> that did not do the source material justice.
