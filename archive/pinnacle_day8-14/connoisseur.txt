# Key:connoisseur
<h3>English Sentences</h3>
E-1: She is a <u>connoisseur</u> of fine wines, always able to identify the distinct flavors.
E-2: As a <u>connoisseur</u> of art, he could appreciate the subtle details in every brushstroke.
