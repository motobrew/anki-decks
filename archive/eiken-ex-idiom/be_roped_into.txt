# Key:be roped into
<h3>English Sentences</h3>
E-1: I didn't want to go, but I was <u>roped into</u> attending the party.
E-2: She always manages to <u>rope me into</u> doing her chores for her.

J-1: 行きたくなかったけど、私はパーティーに<u>強制参加させられた</u>んだ。
