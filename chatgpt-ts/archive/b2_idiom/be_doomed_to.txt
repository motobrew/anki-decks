# Key:be doomed to
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The company's financial troubles may <u>be doomed to</u> bankruptcy.
E-2: Despite his efforts, the project seemed <u>doomed to</u> failure from the beginning.
E-3: The relationship between the two nations may <u>be doomed to</u> tension due to political differences.
