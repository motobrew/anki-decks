# Key:fall through
<h3>English Sentences</h3>
E-1: Our plans to go to the beach <u>fell through</u> due to the bad weather.
E-2: The company's agreement with their supplier <u>fell through</u> at the last minute.

<h3>Japanese Translation</h3>
J-1: 悪天候のため、私たちのビーチへの計画は<u>頓挫しました</u>。
J-2: 会社とサプライヤーの契約は、ギリギリで<u>失敗しました</u>。
