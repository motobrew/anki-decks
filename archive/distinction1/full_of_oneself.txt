# Key:full of oneself
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He's always talking about his achievements, he's so <u>full of himself</u>.
E-2: Don't be so <u>full of yourself</u>, you still have a lot to learn.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼はいつも自分の実績について話していて、自己中心的です。
J-2: 自分に満ち溢れ過ぎないように、まだ学ぶことがたくさんある。
