# Key:in addition to
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: <u>In addition to</u> your salary, you will receive health benefits and a retirement plan.
E-2: <u>In addition to</u> studying for exams, I also have to work part-time to pay for my tuition.
E-3: <u>In addition to</u> English, she is fluent in Spanish and French.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 給料に<u>加えて</u>、健康保険と退職プランも受け取れます。
J-2: 試験勉強<u>に加えて</u>、学費を支払うためにアルバイトもしなければなりません。
J-3: 彼女は英語<u>に加えて</u>、スペイン語とフランス語も流暢です。
