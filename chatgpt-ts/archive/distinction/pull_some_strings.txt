# Key:pull some strings
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I think I can get you that job if I <u>pull some strings</u>.
E-2: He was able to get a reservation at the fully booked restaurant by <u>pulling some strings</u>.
E-3: The CEO was able to secure a huge investment by <u>pulling some strings</u> with his connections.
