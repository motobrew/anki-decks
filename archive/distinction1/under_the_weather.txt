# Key:under the weather
I apologize, but the keyword "under the weather" actually has a specific idiomatic meaning that is different from "pop up." Here are the revised sentences:

### English Sentences
E-1: I can't go out tonight, I'm feeling a bit <u>under the weather</u>.
E-2: She called in sick because she was <u>under the weather</u> with a cold.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 今晩外出できない、ちょっと体調が<u>すぐれない</u>。
J-2: 風邪で<u>具合がすぐれない</u>ので、彼女は休暇を取りました。
