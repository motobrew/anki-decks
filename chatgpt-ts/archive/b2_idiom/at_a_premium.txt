# Key:at a premium
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Nowadays, good customer service is <u>at a premium</u> due to the high demand.
E-2: In the pandemic, medical supplies were <u>at a premium</u> and difficult to acquire.
E-3: In big cities, parking spaces are <u>at a premium</u> and very expensive.
