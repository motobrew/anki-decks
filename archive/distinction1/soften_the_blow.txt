# Key:soften the blow
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The company gave all employees a small bonus to <u>soften the blow</u> of the layoffs.
E-2: The teacher praised Johnny's effort but also suggested areas for improvement to <u>soften the blow</u>.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 会社は全従業員に少額のボーナスを与え、解雇の<u>ダメージを和らげました</u>。
J-2: 先生はジョニーの努力を褒め、改善するべき点も指摘して<u>批判を和らげました</u>。
