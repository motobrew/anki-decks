# Key:town twinning
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Our town is considering <u>town twinning</u> with a city in France.
E-2: <u>Town twinning</u> can promote cultural exchange and understanding between communities.
E-3: Last year, our town hosted a delegation from our twin town in Germany.
