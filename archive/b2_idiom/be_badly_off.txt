# Key:be badly off
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: After losing his job, John found himself <u>badly off</u> and struggling to pay his bills.
E-2: The neighborhood that was once prosperous is now <u>badly off</u> due to the economic downturn.
E-3: Without proper funding, the school will be <u>badly off</u> and unable to support extracurricular activities.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 仕事を失った後、ジョンは<u>困窮</u>し、請求書を支払うのに苦労しました。
J-2: かつて繁栄していた地域は、経済の低迷により<u>窮乏</u>しています。
J-3: 適切な資金を持たなければ、学校は<u>困窮</u>して、課外活動を支援できません。
