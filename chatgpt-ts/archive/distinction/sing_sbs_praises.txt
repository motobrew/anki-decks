# Key:sing sb's praises
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: You always <u>sing your favorite band's praises</u>, but have you ever listened to anything else?
E-2: After her promotion, the boss <u>sang her praises</u> to the entire team.
E-3: The movie critic couldn't help but <u>sing the film's praises</u> in her review.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: いつも好きなバンドの<u>持ち上げ</u>ばかりしてるけど、他に聞いたことある？
J-2: 昇進後、上司はチーム全員に彼女を<u>褒めちぎった</u>。
J-3: 映画評論家はレビューで映画を<u>大絶賛</u>せざるを得ませんでした。
