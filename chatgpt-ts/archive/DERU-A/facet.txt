# Key:facet
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The diamond has many <u>facets</u>, each reflecting the light in a different way.
E-2: The issue has many <u>facets</u>, and we need to consider them all before making a decision.
E-3: Sheila showed us a <u>different facet</u> of her personality when she performed on stage.
