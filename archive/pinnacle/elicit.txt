# Key:elicit
<h3>English Sentences</h3>
E-1: The teacher asked a question to <u>elicit</u> a response from the students.
E-2: The detective tried to <u>elicit</u> information from the suspect during interrogation.

<h3>Japanese Translation</h3>
J-1: 先生は質問をすることで生徒から<u>反応を引き出しました</u>。
J-2: 刑事は尋問中に容疑者から情報を<u>引き出そうとしました</u>。
