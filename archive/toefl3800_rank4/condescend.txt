# Key:condescend
### English Sentences
E-1: She has a tendency to <u>condescend</u> when speaking to her employees.
E-2: The professor would <u>condescend</u> to explain basic concepts to his students.

J-1: 彼女は従業員と話す時に<u>高圧的な態度</u>を取る傾向があります。
