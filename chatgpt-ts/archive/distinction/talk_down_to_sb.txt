# Key:talk down to sb
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Don't <u>talk down to me</u> just because I'm younger than you.
E-2: The boss always <u>talks down to his employees</u> and it's really frustrating.
E-3: It's not polite to <u>talk down to someone</u> just because they don't speak English well.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 年下の私に対して<u>嫌味を言うのはやめて</u>ください。
J-2: 上司はいつも部下に<u>威張り散らして</u>いてとても腹が立ちます。
J-3: 英語が上手く話せないからといって、<u>人を見下したりしないで</u>ください。
