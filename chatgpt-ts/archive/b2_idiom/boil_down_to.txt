# Key:boil down to
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: At the end of the day, success <u>boils down to</u> hard work and dedication.
E-2: The argument <u>boiled down to</u> a disagreement over finances.
E-3: The final decision will <u>boil down to</u> which option is most cost-effective.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 結局のところ、成功は努力と献身に<u>帰結する</u>。
J-2: 論争は財政の不一致に<u>帰着した</u>。
J-3: 最終決定は、最もコスト効果的なオプションを<u>帰結する</u>。
