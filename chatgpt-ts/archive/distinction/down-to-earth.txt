# Key:down-to-earth
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I really like hanging out with Sarah because she's so <u>down-to-earth</u> and easy to talk to.
E-2: My neighbor drives a fancy sports car, but he's surprisingly <u>down-to-earth</u> and always willing to lend a hand.
E-3: The new boss seems very <u>down-to-earth</u> and approachable, which is a relief after working with the last one.
