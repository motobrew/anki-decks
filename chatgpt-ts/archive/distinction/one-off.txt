# Key:one-off
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: That concert was amazing, but unfortunately it was just a <u>one-off</u> event.
E-2: I found a rare vinyl record at a garage sale, it's a <u>one-off</u> pressing!
E-3: I'm not worried about failing this exam, it's just a <u>one-off</u> mistake.

J-1: あのコンサートは素晴らしかったけど、残念ながらそれは単発のイベントだった。
J-2: ガレージセールでレアなビニール盤を見つけた、それは単発のプレスだ！
J-3: この試験に失敗する心配はない、単発のミスだから。
