# Key:heads-up
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Hey, <u>heads-up</u>! The boss is coming this way.
E-2: I just wanted to give you a <u>heads-up</u> that the concert starts at 8 PM, not 7 PM.
E-3: Can you give me a <u>heads-up</u> if you're going to be late for the meeting?

### Japanese Translation
J-1: おい、<u>気をつけろ</u>！上司がこの方向に来てるぞ。
J-2: コンサートは7時じゃなくて8時からだよ、<u>念のためお知らせ</u>したかったんだ。
J-3: 会議に遅れる場合は、<u>事前に知らせて</u>くれる？
