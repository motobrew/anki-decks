# Key:crash down
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: As soon as the earthquake hit, the walls started to <u>crash down</u>.
E-2: The strong wind made the trees <u>crash down</u> onto the road.
E-3: The tower was so tall that when it finally collapsed, it let out a loud <u>crash down</u>.
