# Key:set forth
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The company will <u>set forth</u> a new plan for expansion next month.
E-2: The author <u>set forth</u> a compelling argument in their latest book.
E-3: The professor <u>set forth</u> the rules for the research project at the beginning of the semester.
