# Key:incredulous
### English Sentences
E-1: She looked at him with an <u>incredulous</u> expression, unable to believe what he was saying.
E-2: The students were <u>incredulous</u> when they heard that school was cancelled.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼が言っていることを信じられないという<u>不信な</u>表情で彼女は彼を見つめた。
J-2: 学校が休みになったと聞いて、生徒たちは<u>信じられない</u>と驚いた。
