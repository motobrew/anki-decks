# Key:walk on sunshine
## Response

E-1: She's so happy she's practically <u>walking on sunshine</u>!
E-2: After getting a promotion at work, I feel like I'm <u>walking on sunshine</u>.
E-3: Despite the bad news, he's <u>walking on sunshine</u> and feeling optimistic.
