# Key:think much of
## Response

E-1: I don't really <u>think much of</u> his art style, but some people seem to like it.
E-2: She doesn't <u>think much of</u> his cooking skills, so she always prepares the meals herself.
E-3: The boss doesn't <u>think much of</u> the new employee's work ethic, and is considering firing them.
