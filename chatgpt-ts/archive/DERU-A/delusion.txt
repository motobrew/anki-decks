# Key:delusion
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: His belief that he could fly was a <u>delusion</u>.
E-2: She was suffering from a <u>delusion</u> that everyone was out to get her.
E-3: The cult leader's <u>delusions</u> led to the mass suicide of his followers.
