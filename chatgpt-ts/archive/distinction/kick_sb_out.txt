# Key:kick sb out
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The bouncer had to <u>kick</u> that unruly patron <u>out</u> of the bar.
E-2: If you don't behave yourself, I'll have to <u>kick you out</u> of class.
E-3: The landlord decided to <u>kick the tenants out</u> for not paying rent.
