# Key:waver
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: His confidence in his decision began to <u>waver</u> when he heard opposing arguments.
E-2: The company's profits have started to <u>waver</u> due to increased competition.
E-3: She tried not to <u>waver</u> in her commitment to her goals, even in the face of obstacles.

J-1: 反対意見を聞いた時、彼の決断に対する自信が揺らぎ始めた。

J-3: 彼女は障害に直面しても、自分の目標への取り組みを揺るがさせないように努めた。
