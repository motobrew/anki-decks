# Key:unheard of
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The fact that she's a princess and I'm just a commoner is <u>unheard of</u>.
E-2: Having a wedding ceremony without exchanging vows is <u>unheard of</u>.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼女がお姫様で私がただの庶民だというのは<u>前代未聞</u>です。
J-2: 誓いを交わさずに結婚式を行うなんて<u>あり得ないこと</u>です。
