# Key:take a beating
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: After playing in the football game, my body felt like it had <u>taken a beating</u>.
E-2: The stock market has <u>taken a beating</u> this week due to economic uncertainty.
E-3: The old car had <u>taken a beating</u> over the years and needed many repairs.
