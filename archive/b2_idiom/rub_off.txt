# Key:rub off
## Response

E-1: The color of your shirt <u>rubs off</u> on my skin.
E-2: Her enthusiasm for the project really <u>rubs off</u> on everyone around her.
E-3: If you spend enough time with him, his bad habits might <u>rub off</u> on you.
