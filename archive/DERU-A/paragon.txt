# Key:paragon
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Mary is a <u>paragon</u> of beauty and grace, admired by all who know her.
E-2: The young athlete is considered a <u>paragon</u> of dedication and hard work by his coaches.
E-3: The CEO is often regarded as a <u>paragon</u> of success in the business world.

J-1: メアリーは美しさと優雅さの<u>模範</u>であり、彼女を知るすべての人に賞賛されています。
