# Key:wordsmith sth
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I heard that John is a <u>wordsmith</u> when it comes to writing poetry.
E-2: My cousin is a talented <u>wordsmith</u> and always knows the right thing to say.
E-3: The new advertising campaign was a success thanks to the <u>wordsmithing</u> of the copywriter.

J-1: ジョンは詩を書くときには<u>言葉の職人</u>だと聞きました。

J-3: 広告キャンペーンが成功したのは、コピーライターの<u>言葉遣い</u>のおかげです。
