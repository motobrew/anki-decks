# Key:gray area
Since the keyword "gray area" has a single meaning, there is no need for different interpretations. 

## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The legality of the situation is in a <u>gray area</u>, so we should consult a lawyer. 
E-2: His behavior falls into a <u>gray area</u>, not clearly right or wrong.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: その状況の合法性は<u>曖昧な状況</u>にあるため、弁護士に相談すべきです。
J-2: 彼の行動は明確には<u>正しいでもなく、間違っているでもない</u>曖昧な状況にある。
