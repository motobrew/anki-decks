# Key:smooth things over
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I made a mistake in the presentation, but I was able to <u>smooth things over</u> with a joke.
E-2: Our argument was getting heated, but we were able to <u>smooth things over</u> by talking it out.
E-3: The boss was angry about the missed deadline, but the team was able to <u>smooth things over</u> by offering to work overtime.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: プレゼンテーションでミスをしたけど、ジョークで事を<u>収める</u>ことができた。
J-2: 私たちの議論は激しくなっていたけど、話し合いで<u>妥協点</u>を見つけることができた。
J-3: 上司は期限を守れなかったことで怒っていたけど、チームは残業を申し出ることで<u>取り繕う</u>ことができた。
