# Key:file a suit
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The plaintiff plans to <u>file a suit</u> against the company for breach of contract.
E-2: The defendant's lawyer advised him to <u>file a suit</u> to protect his rights.
E-3: It can be expensive to <u>file a suit</u>, but sometimes it's necessary to seek legal action.
