# Key:ease up
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: <u>Ease up</u> on yourself and take a break once in a while.
E-2: Can you <u>ease up</u> on the volume of the music? It's too loud.
E-3: The coach told the player to <u>ease up</u> on his training, to avoid injury.
