# Key:tinge
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The painting had a <u>tinge</u> of blue in the background.
E-2: Her voice had a <u>tinge</u> of sadness when she spoke about her childhood.
E-3: The sun's rays gave the clouds a <u>tinge</u> of pink at sunset.

J-1: 絵には背景に青い<u>色合い</u>があった。

J-3: 夕陽の時、太陽の光線は雲に<u>ピンク色の色合い</u>を与えた。
