# Key:marginally
### English Sentences
E-1: The price of the product was only marginally reduced during the sale.
E-2: His performance improved only marginally after receiving additional training.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: セール中に商品の価格は<u>わずかに</u>値下げされました。
J-2: 追加のトレーニングを受けた後も彼のパフォーマンスは<u>わずかに</u>改善しました。
