# Key:furtively
### English Sentences
E-1: She glanced around <u>furtively</u> before slipping the stolen necklace into her purse.
E-2: He <u>furtively</u> texted his friend during the important meeting.

J-1: 彼女は<u>こっそり</u>周りを見回して、盗んだネックレスをバッグにしのばせました。
