# Key:OCD
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: My boss is so <u>OCD</u> about keeping the office clean and organized.
E-2: I can't leave the house without checking everything twice, it's like my brain has <u>OCD</u>.
E-3: She's so <u>OCD</u> about her diet, she weighs every single thing she eats.

J-1: 上司はオフィスをきれいで整然とさせることにとても<u>こだわりが強い</u>。

J-3: 彼女は食事にとても<u>こだわりが強くて</u>、食べるものごとに計量器で量ってしまう。
