# Key:control freak
## Response

E-1: My boss is a total <u>control freak</u>, she wants to micromanage every aspect of the project.
E-2: I can't stand being in a relationship with a <u>control freak</u>, they always want everything their way.
E-3: My roommate is a <u>control freak</u> about cleanliness, he gets upset if I don't clean the dishes right away.
