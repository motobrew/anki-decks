# Key:edge out
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The company was looking to <u>edge out</u> its competitors by launching a new advertising campaign.
E-2: The reigning champion was <u>edged out</u> by a newcomer in the final round of the boxing match.
E-3: The CEO of the company was planning to <u>edge out</u> his partner and become the sole owner of the business.
