# Key:rummage
### English Sentences
E-1: I decided to <u>rummage</u> through my closet to find my lost keys.
E-2: The detective started to <u>rummage</u> through the suspect's drawers to look for evidence.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 鍵を探すために、私はクローゼットを<u>くまなく探しました</u>。
J-2: 刑事は証拠を探すために、容疑者の引き出しを<u>くまなく捜査し始めました</u>。
