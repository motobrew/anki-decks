# Key:absolve
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The priest <u>absolved</u> the sinner of his sins after confession.
E-2: The court <u>absolved</u> the accused of all charges due to lack of evidence.
E-3: I can't <u>absolve</u> you of your responsibilities, you have to face the consequences.
