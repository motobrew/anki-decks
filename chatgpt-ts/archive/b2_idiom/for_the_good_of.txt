# Key:for the good of
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The company has decided to lay off some employees <u>for the good of</u> the business.
E-2: The government is implementing stricter regulations <u>for the good of</u> public health.
E-3: Sometimes it's necessary to make sacrifices <u>for the good of</u> the greater community.
