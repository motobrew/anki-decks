# Key:pull off
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He was able to <u>pull off</u> an amazing card trick in front of the audience.
E-2: She wanted to <u>pull off</u> the heist without getting caught by the security.
E-3: The team was determined to <u>pull off</u> a victory in the championship game.
