# Key:drive sb up the wall
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: His constant humming is driving me up the wall.
E-2: My sister's messy room always drives my mom up the wall.
E-3: Waiting in long lines at the amusement park can drive anyone up the wall.

J-3: アミューズメントパークで長い列に並ぶことは誰でも<u>イライラ</u>させます。
