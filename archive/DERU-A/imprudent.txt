# Key:imprudent
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: It would be <u>imprudent</u> to invest all your savings in one risky business venture.
E-2: The politician's <u>imprudent</u> actions caused a lot of controversy and backlash.
E-3: It's important to avoid <u>imprudent</u> spending habits in order to maintain financial stability.
