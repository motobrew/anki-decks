# Key:impeccable
### English Sentences
E-1: Her taste in fashion is <u>impeccable</u>; she always looks elegant.
E-2: The chef's culinary skills are <u>impeccable</u>; every dish is perfection.

J-2: そのシェフの料理の腕前は<u>完璧</u>です。どの料理も完璧な出来栄えです。
