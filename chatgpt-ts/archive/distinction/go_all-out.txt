# Key:go all-out
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: We decided to <u>go all-out</u> for her surprise party and rented a yacht for the occasion.
E-2: The team is going to <u>go all-out</u> to win this championship game.
E-3: My brother always <u>goes all-out</u> for Halloween decorations and turns our house into a haunted mansion.
