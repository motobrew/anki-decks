# Key:bestow
### English Sentences
E-1: The university <u>bestowed</u> an honorary degree upon the renowned scientist.
E-2: The queen <u>bestowed</u> a knighthood upon the actor for his contributions to the arts.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: その有名な科学者に大学は名誉学位を<u>授与しました</u>。
J-2: 女王はその俳優に芸術への貢献を称えて爵位を<u>与えました</u>。
