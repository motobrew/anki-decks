# Key:at a standstill
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The negotiations between the two companies are <u>at a standstill</u> due to disagreements over the pricing.
E-2: After the earthquake, the traffic on the highway was <u>at a standstill</u> for hours.
E-3: The construction of the new building is <u>at a standstill</u> until the permits are approved.
