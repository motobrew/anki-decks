# Key:bear fruit
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: With hard work and dedication, your efforts will eventually <u>bear fruit</u>.
E-2: Mary's good behavior will <u>bear fruit</u> as her parents will reward her with a trip to Disneyland.
E-3: The company's strategy to enter the Chinese market is starting to <u>bear fruit</u> with their successful sales numbers.
