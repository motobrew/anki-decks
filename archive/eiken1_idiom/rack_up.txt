# Key:rack up
<h3>English Sentences</h3>
E-1: The basketball player <u>racked up</u> 30 points in the game.
E-2: I'm going to <u>rack up</u> some credit card debt if I keep shopping like this.

J-1: バスケットボール選手は試合で30点<u>を獲得しました</u>。
