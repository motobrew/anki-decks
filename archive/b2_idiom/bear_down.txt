# Key:bear down
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He had to <u>bear down</u> and focus in order to finish the difficult task.
E-2: The team needed to <u>bear down</u> and work harder to win the championship.
E-3: During the storm, we had to <u>bear down</u> and hold onto something sturdy.
