# Key:treading water
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: My workload is so heavy, I feel like I'm just <u>treading water</u>.
E-2: Ever since his business started failing, he's been <u>treading water</u> financially.
E-3: I'm just <u>treading water</u> in this dead-end job.

J-1: 仕事が多忙で、ただ<中身がない>状態に陥っている気がします。

J-3: この行き止まりの仕事でただ<立ち止まっている>だけです。
