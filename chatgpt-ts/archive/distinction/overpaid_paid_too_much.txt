# Key:overpaid paid too much
## Response

E-1: That CEO is ridiculously <u>overpaid</u> for his mediocre performance.
E-2: I can't believe they're offering such a low salary for such an important job - they must think we're all <u>overpaid</u>.
E-3: The athletes might be <u>overpaid</u>, but they have to work incredibly hard to make it to that level.
