# Key:sketchy
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: That bar looks <u>sketchy</u>, let's find somewhere else to drink tonight.
E-2: I don't want to buy that used car, something about the seller seems <u>sketchy</u>.
E-3: The whole situation seemed <u>sketchy</u>, so I decided not to get involved.
