# Key:commotion
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: There was a lot of <u>commotion</u> outside the stadium after the game ended.
E-2: The sudden <u>commotion</u> in the classroom made it difficult for the teacher to continue the lesson.
E-3: The arrival of the celebrity caused a <u>commotion</u> among the fans waiting outside the hotel.
