# Key:right off
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The car dealership offered to take $2,000 <u>right off</u> the price of the car.
E-2: I knew <u>right off</u> the bat that the job was not right for me.
E-3: She was able to answer the question <u>right off</u> the top of her head.
