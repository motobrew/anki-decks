# Key:hold sth/sb up
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The traffic jam <u>held me up</u>, so I'll be a little late.
E-2: The bad weather <u>held up</u> our flight for several hours.
E-3: The construction work <u>held up</u> the delivery of our new furniture.

J-1: 交通渋滞で<u>遅れてしまった</u>ので、少しだけ遅れます。

J-3: 建設工事が原因で、新しい家具の<u>配送が遅れた</u>。
