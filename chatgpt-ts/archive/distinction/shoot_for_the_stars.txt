# Key:shoot for the stars
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: You should always <u>shoot for the stars</u> when setting your goals.
E-2: He's not afraid to <u>shoot for the stars</u> when it comes to his career aspirations.
E-3: I know it's a big risk, but let's <u>shoot for the stars</u> and see what happens.
