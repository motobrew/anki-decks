# Key:mock
### English Sentences
E-1: She <u>mocked</u> his terrible singing by mimicking his off-key notes. 
E-2: The bully always <u>mocks</u> the other students, making fun of their appearance. 

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼女は彼のひどい歌声を<u>あざ笑い</u>、音程の外れた音をまねました。
J-2: そのいじめっ子はいつも他の生徒たちを<u>嘲笑い</u>、容姿をからかいます。
