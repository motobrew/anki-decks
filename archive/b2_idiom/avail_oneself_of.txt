# Key:avail oneself of
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: When studying for exams, it's important to <u>avail oneself of</u> all available resources.
E-2: You should <u>avail oneself of</u> the opportunity to travel abroad while you are young.
E-3: The company offers employees the chance to <u>avail themselves of</u> a gym membership.
