# Key:kick sth off
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Let's <u>kick off</u> the meeting with a quick update on progress.
E-2: The concert will <u>kick off</u> at 8 PM sharp, so don't be late.
E-3: We're planning to <u>kick off</u> the party with some fun icebreaker games.
