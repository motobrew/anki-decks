# Key:notify A of B
## Response

E-1: Can you please <u>notify John</u> of the meeting time and location?
E-2: The landlord needs to <u>notify the tenants</u> of upcoming renovations in the building.
E-3: The company will <u>notify its customers</u> of new product releases via email.
