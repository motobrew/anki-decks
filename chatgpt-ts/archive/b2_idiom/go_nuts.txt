# Key:go nuts
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: When the new Star Wars movie comes out, I'm going to <u>go nuts</u> with excitement.
E-2: My sister <u>went nuts</u> when she found out she got accepted into her dream college.
E-3: If my boss gives me one more last-minute assignment, I'm going to <u>go nuts</u>.

J-3: もし上司がもう一つの締め切りギリギリの課題を与えたら、<u>我慢できなくなる</u>。
