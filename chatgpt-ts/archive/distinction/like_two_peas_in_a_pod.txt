# Key:like two peas in a pod
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: John and his sister are like <u>two peas in a pod</u>, they have the same sense of humor.
E-2: Those twins are like <u>two peas in a pod</u>, I can never tell them apart.
E-3: My grandparents have been married for 60 years and are still like <u>two peas in a pod</u>.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: ジョンと彼の妹は、ユーモアのセンスが同じで、まるで<u>瓜二つだ</u>。
J-2: あの双子は、<u>瓜二つで</u>、いつも見分けがつきません。
J-3: 私の祖父母は結婚して60年経ちますが、未だに<u>瓜二つのように</u>仲良しです。
