# Key:eat away
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The acid will eventually <u>eat away</u> the metal if left unchecked.
E-2: Every time I eat ice cream, it feels like it's <u>eating away</u> at my teeth.
E-3: The high cost of living is slowly <u>eating away</u> at my savings.
