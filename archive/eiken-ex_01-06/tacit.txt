# Key:tacit
### English Sentences
E-1: The <u>tacit</u> agreement between the two countries allowed for peaceful negotiations.
E-2: Our friendship is built on a <u>tacit</u> understanding of each other's boundaries.

J-1: 2つの国の<u>暗黙の</u>合意により、平和的な交渉が可能となりました。
