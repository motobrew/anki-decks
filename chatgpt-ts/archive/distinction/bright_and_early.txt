# Key:bright and early
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I have to wake up <u>bright and early</u> tomorrow for my flight.
E-2: The farmers start work <u>bright and early</u> to take advantage of the cool morning air.
E-3: The store opens <u>bright and early</u> at 7 AM for the Black Friday sale.

J-3: ブラックフライデーのセールのため、店は<u>早朝</u>7時にオープンする。
