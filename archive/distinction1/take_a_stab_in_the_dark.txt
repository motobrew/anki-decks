# Key:take a stab in the dark
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I'm not sure about the answer, but I'll <u>take a stab in the dark</u>.
E-2: Without any information, it's foolish to <u>take a stab in the dark</u>.

J-1: 答えが分からないけど、<u>推測してみる</u>よ。
J-2: 情報がないのに、<u>見当を付けるのは愚かだ</u>。
