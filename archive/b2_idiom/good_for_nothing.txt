# Key:good for nothing
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He's good for nothing and never does any work around here.
E-2: That old computer is good for nothing and needs to be thrown away.
E-3: Her ex-boyfriend was a good for nothing who never treated her well.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼は何の役にも立たず、ここでは仕事をしない。
J-2: あの古いコンピューターは役に立たないので、捨てる必要がある。
J-3: 彼女の元カレは何の役にも立たず、彼女を悪く扱っていた。
