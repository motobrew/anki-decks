# Key:deciduous tree
### English Sentences
E-1: The changing colors of the leaves indicate that fall has arrived, and the <u>deciduous trees</u> are shedding their leaves.
E-2: The landscape will look bare in winter because the <u>deciduous trees</u> have lost all their leaves.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 葉っぱの色の変化から秋が訪れ、落葉樹が葉を落としていることを示しています。
J-2: 冬になると、落葉樹は葉を全て失い、景色は寂しくなります。
