# Key:with a vengeance
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: After losing the championship last year, the team came back <u>with a vengeance</u> and won every game this season.
E-2: The company's new CEO is making changes <u>with a vengeance</u>, restructuring the entire organization.
E-3: After being rejected from the school's drama club last year, she auditioned again <u>with a vengeance</u> and landed the lead role.
