# Key:wipe out
<h3>English Sentences</h3>
E-1: The storm <u>wiped out</u> the entire village, leaving it devastated.
E-2: He invested all his savings in that business and it <u>wiped him out</u>.

J-1: 嵐がその村全体を<u>壊滅させ</u>、取り残されました。
