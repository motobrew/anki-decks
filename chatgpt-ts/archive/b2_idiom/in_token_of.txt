# Key:in token of
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He gave her a bouquet of flowers <u>in token of</u> his affection.
E-2: The company gave its employees a bonus <u>in token of</u> their hard work.
E-3: The queen presented the knight with a medal <u>in token of</u> his bravery.

J-1: 彼は愛情の<u>しるしに</u>彼女に花束を贈った。
