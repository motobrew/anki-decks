# Key:catch A's eye
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Her brightly colored outfit <u>caught A's eye</u> as she walked down the street.
E-2: The unique design of the building <u>caught A's eye</u> and drew her closer to take a look.
E-3: The impressive resume <u>caught A's eye</u> and made her stand out among the other job applicants.
