# Key:black sheep
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He's always been the <u>black sheep</u> of the family, always getting into trouble.
E-2: The company doesn't trust him because he's seen as the <u>black sheep</u> of the department.
E-3: She feels like the <u>black sheep</u> in her group of friends because she doesn't share their interests.
