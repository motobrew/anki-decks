# Key:tentative
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The plans for the project are still <u>tentative</u> and subject to change.
E-2: The couple made a <u>tentative</u> agreement to meet for dinner on Friday.
E-3: The company made a <u>tentative</u> offer to buy out their smaller competitor.
