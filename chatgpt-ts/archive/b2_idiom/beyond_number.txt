# Key:beyond number
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The magnitude of the problem is <u>beyond number</u>, and will require a collective effort to solve.
E-2: The beauty of the stars in the dark sky is <u>beyond number</u>, and fills me with wonder.
E-3: The amount of data we collected from the experiment is <u>beyond number</u>, and will take weeks to process.
