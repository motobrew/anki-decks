# Key:meet sb halfway
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I know we have different opinions, but let's <u>meet halfway</u> and find a solution.
E-2: Can you <u>meet me halfway</u> between our houses for our morning run?

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 意見が違うけれど、<u>妥協</u>して解決策を見つけましょう。
J-2: 朝のランニングのために、家と家の<u>中間地点</u>で会えますか？
