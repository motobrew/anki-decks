# Key:spit it out
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: If you have something to say, just <u>spit it out</u>. 
E-2: I can't understand you when you mumble like that, please <u>spit it out</u>.
E-3: He was hesitant to admit it, but finally decided to <u>spit it out</u>.

J-3: 彼は言うのをためらったが、最終的には<u>言い出した</u>。
