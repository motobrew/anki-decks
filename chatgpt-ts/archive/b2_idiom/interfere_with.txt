# Key:interfere with
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Loud music can <u>interfere with</u> your concentration while studying.
E-2: Please don't <u>interfere with</u> my work, I need to focus.
E-3: The new construction will <u>interfere with</u> the traffic patterns.
