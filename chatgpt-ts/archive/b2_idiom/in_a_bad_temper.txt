# Key:in a bad temper
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: She was in a bad temper after the argument with her boss.
E-2: He's been in a bad temper all day, I think he just needs some space.
E-3: The customer was in a bad temper and demanded to speak to the manager.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼女は上司との口論の後、<u>機嫌が悪かった</u>。
J-2: 彼は一日中<u>不機嫌だった</u>。彼は単にスペースが必要なだけだと思います。
J-3: その客は<u>機嫌が悪かった</u>ので、マネージャーに話すよう要求した。
