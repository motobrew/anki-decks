# Key:get one's hopes up
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I don't want to <u>get my hopes up</u> about winning the lottery, but it's hard not to dream.
E-2: My boss said she'd consider me for the promotion, but I don't want to <u>get my hopes up</u> too much.
E-3: I was excited about the party, but then I found out it got cancelled so now I'm trying not to <u>get my hopes up</u> about rescheduling.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 宝くじで勝つことに<u>期待し過ぎる</u>わけじゃないけど、夢を見るのは止められない。
J-2: 上司は昇進のことを考えてくれると言ってくれたけど、あまり<u>期待し過ぎたくない</u>。
J-3: パーティーについて楽しみにしていたけど、その後中止になったので今度計画し直すことに<u>期待し過ぎない</u>ようにしている。
