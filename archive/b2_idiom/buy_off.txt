# Key:buy off
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The company tried to <u>buy off</u> the protestors with a large sum of money.
E-2: I won't be <u>buying off</u> your silence, so you better keep your mouth shut.
E-3: The politician was accused of attempting to <u>buy off</u> his opponents.
