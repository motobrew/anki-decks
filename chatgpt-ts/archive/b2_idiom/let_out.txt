# Key:let out
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: When she heard the joke, she couldn't help but <u>let out</u> a loud laugh.
E-2: My dress is too tight, I need to <u>let out</u> the seams a bit.
E-3: The teacher was angry and <u>let out</u> a loud yell.
