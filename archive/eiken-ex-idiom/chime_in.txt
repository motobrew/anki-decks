# Key:chime in
<h3>English Sentences</h3>
E-1: I heard someone <u>chime in</u> with their opinion during the meeting.
E-2: As soon as I mentioned the topic, she decided to <u>chime in</u> with her own experience.

<h3>Japanese Translation</h3>
J-1: 会議中、誰かが自分の意見を<u>出しました</u>。
J-2: 私がその話題を出すとすぐに、彼女は<u>自分の経験を話し始めました</u>。
