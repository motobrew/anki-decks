# Key:burgeon
### English Sentences
E-1: The number of tourists visiting the city began to <u>burgeon</u> after the new airport was built.
E-2: The popularity of the singer continued to <u>burgeon</u> as more people discovered their music.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 新しい空港が建設された後、その都市を訪れる観光客の数が<u>急増し始めました</u>。
J-2: シンガーの人気は、より多くの人々が彼らの音楽に出会うにつれて<u>ますます高まりました</u>。
