# Key:far cry from
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The luxurious suite at the five-star hotel is a <u>far cry from</u> the cramped hostel I stayed at last year.
E-2: Her cooking skills are still a <u>far cry from</u> professional chef-level, but she's improving.
E-3: The new movie adaptation was a <u>far cry from</u> the original book in terms of accuracy.
