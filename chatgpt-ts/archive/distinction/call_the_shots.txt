# Key:call the shots
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: She's the CEO of the company and <u>calls the shots</u> on all major decisions.
E-2: The coach lets the star player <u>call the shots</u> during the game.
E-3: The boss wants to <u>call the shots</u> on this project, even though it's not his area of expertise.
