# Key:the long and the short of it
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The long and the short of it is that we need to increase profits if we want to stay in business.
E-2: The long and the short of it is that I'm not interested in going to the party tonight.
E-3: The long and the short of it is that we need to find a new apartment before our lease ends.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 言うまでもなく、ビジネスを維持するために利益を増やす必要があります。
J-2: 結局のところ、今夜のパーティーには興味がありません。
J-3: 結局のところ、リース期限が切れる前に新しいアパートを見つける必要があります。
