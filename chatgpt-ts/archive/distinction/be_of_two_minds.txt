# Key:be of two minds
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I'm <u>of two minds</u> about whether to take that job offer or not.
E-2: She's <u>of two minds</u> about which college to attend.
E-3: He's <u>of two minds</u> whether to stay in or go out tonight.
