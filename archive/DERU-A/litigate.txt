# Key:litigate
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The two companies decided to <u>litigate</u> their contract dispute in court.
E-2: It can be expensive to <u>litigate</u> a case, so it's important to consider alternative dispute resolution methods.
E-3: The lawyer advised his clients to try to settle the dispute out of court, rather than <u>litigate</u> for months or even years.
