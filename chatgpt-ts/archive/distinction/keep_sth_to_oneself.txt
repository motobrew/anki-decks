# Key:keep sth to oneself
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He found out who the thief was but decided to <u>keep it to himself</u>.
E-2: She knew the answer to the question but chose to <u>keep it to herself</u>.
E-3: Don't <u>keep your feelings to yourself</u>, share them with someone you trust.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 盗人が誰だか彼は分かったけど、自分だけで<u>秘密にしておいた</u>。
J-2: 彼女はその質問の答えを知っていたけど、<u>自分だけの秘密にしておいた</u>。
J-3: <u>感情を自分だけに抱え込まないで</u>、信頼できる人と共有してください。
