# Key:piece together
<h3>English Sentences</h3>
E-1: I had to <u>piece together</u> the puzzle to see the complete picture.
E-2: The detective tried to <u>piece together</u> the clues to solve the crime.

J-1: 私はパズルを<u>組み合わせて</u>全体の絵を見る必要がありました。
