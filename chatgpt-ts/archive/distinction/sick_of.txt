# Key:sick of
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I'm really <u>sick of</u> my job, it's so boring.
E-2: He's <u>sick of</u> hearing the same song on the radio every day.
E-3: She's <u>sick of</u> her roommate always leaving a mess in the kitchen.
