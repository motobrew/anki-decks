# Key:irreparably
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The damage to the computer was <u>irreparably</u> done, and we need to buy a new one.
E-2: The fire <u>irreparably</u> damaged the historical building, and it had to be torn down.
E-3: The betrayal by her best friend <u>irreparably</u> damaged their relationship, and they never spoke again.
