# Key:up in the air
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The plans for the weekend are still <u>up in the air</u>, we haven't decided yet.
E-2: My job situation is <u>up in the air</u> right now, I don't know if I'll be laid off or not.
E-3: The future of the project is <u>up in the air</u>, we're waiting for more funding.
