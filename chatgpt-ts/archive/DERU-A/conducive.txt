# Key:conducive
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: A quiet atmosphere is <u>conducive</u> to studying.
E-2: This room's layout is <u>conducive</u> to hosting large parties.
E-3: The warm weather is <u>conducive</u> to outdoor activities like hiking and swimming.
