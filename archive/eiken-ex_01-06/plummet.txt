# Key:plummet
### English Sentences
E-1: The stock prices <u>plummeted</u> after the company announced its bankruptcy.
E-2: The temperature <u>plummeted</u> to below freezing overnight, causing icy road conditions.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: その会社が倒産を発表した後、株価は<u>急落しました</u>。
J-2: 一晩で気温が氷点下まで<u>急落し、凍結した道路状況を引き起こしました</u>。
