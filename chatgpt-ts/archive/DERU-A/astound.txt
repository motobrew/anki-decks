# Key:astound
## Response

E-1: The magician's performance <u>astounded</u> the audience with its incredible illusions.
E-2: The beautiful scenery of the mountains never fails to <u>astound</u> me.
E-3: The sheer size of the whale <u>astounded</u> the researchers who were studying it.
