# Key:take heart
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: <u>Take heart</u>, things will get better eventually.
E-2: Even though we lost the game, we can <u>take heart</u> in knowing that we played our best.
E-3: In times of difficulty, it's important to <u>take heart</u> and keep going.
