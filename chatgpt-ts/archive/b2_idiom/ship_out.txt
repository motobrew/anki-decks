# Key:ship out
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: We need to <u>ship out</u> these products by the end of the week to meet the deadline.
E-2: After completing his training, John will <u>ship out</u> to his new assignment overseas.
E-3: The company will <u>ship out</u> new orders starting next month.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 締め切りに間に合わせるため、この製品を今週末までに<u>発送</u>する必要があります。
J-2: トレーニングを終えたら、ジョンは異動先の海外に<u>出発する</u>予定です。
J-3: 会社は来月から新規受注を<u>発送</u>します。
