# Key:tie into
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The theme of the conference will <u>tie into</u> the latest research in the field. 
E-2: The movie's plot will <u>tie into</u> the previous installment of the franchise. 
E-3: The company's new product will <u>tie into</u> their overall strategy for growth.
