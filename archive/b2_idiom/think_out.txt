# Key:think out
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He always takes a break to <u>think out</u> his next move before making a decision.
E-2: The inventor had to <u>think out</u> every detail in his design before presenting it to the investors.
E-3: The teacher asked the students to <u>think out</u> their answers carefully before responding.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼は常に決定をする前に次の動きを<u>じっくり考える</u>ために休憩をとります。
J-2: 発明家は投資家に提出する前に彼のデザインのすべての詳細を<u>練り上げる</u>必要がありました。
J-3: 先生は学生に、回答する前に<u>注意深く考え抜く</u>ように頼みました。
