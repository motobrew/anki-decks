# Key:be proficient in
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: To become a successful programmer, you need to <u>be proficient in</u> coding languages like Java and Python.
E-2: In order to pursue a career in architecture, one should <u>be proficient in</u> design software like AutoCAD and SketchUp.
E-3: As a translator, it is essential to <u>be proficient in</u> at least two languages.
