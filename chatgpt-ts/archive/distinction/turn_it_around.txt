# Key:turn it around
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He was struggling with his weight but was able to <u>turn it around</u> with a healthier lifestyle.
E-2: The company was failing but the new CEO was able to <u>turn it around</u> with new strategies.
E-3: She used to be a troublemaker, but after going to rehab she was able to <u>turn it around</u> and get her life on track.
