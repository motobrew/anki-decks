# Key:inquire into
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The government will <u>inquire into</u> the allegations of corruption against the company.
E-2: The committee was formed to <u>inquire into</u> the cause of the accident.
E-3: The teacher will <u>inquire into</u> the student's absence from class.
