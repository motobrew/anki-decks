# Key:dearth
## Response

E-1: There is a <u>dearth</u> of food supplies in this famine-stricken region.
E-2: The <u>dearth</u> of rain has resulted in drought across the country.
E-3: The pandemic has caused a <u>dearth</u> of job opportunities.
