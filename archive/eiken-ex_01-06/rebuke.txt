# Key:rebuke
### English Sentences
E-1: He received a <u>rebuke</u> from his boss for constantly arriving late.
E-2: The teacher gave a strong <u>rebuke</u> to the students for cheating on the test.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼は常に遅刻することで上司から<u>叱責</u>を受けた。
J-2: 先生は試験でカンニングをした生徒たちに<u>厳しい叱責</u>をした。
