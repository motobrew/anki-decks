# Key:take root
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The idea of starting his own business <u>took root</u> in his mind.
E-2: It may take awhile for these plants to <u>take root</u> in the new soil.
E-3: The tradition of celebrating Thanksgiving has <u>taken root</u> in many cultures around the world.

J-1: 彼自身でビジネスを始めるというアイデアが彼の頭に<u>定着しました</u>。

J-3: 感謝祭を祝う伝統は、世界中の多くの文化において<u>根付いています</u>。
