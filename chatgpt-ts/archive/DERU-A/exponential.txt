# Key:exponential
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The population of the city has been growing <u>exponentially</u> in the past decade.
E-2: The company's profits have been increasing <u>exponentially</u> since they introduced their new product.
E-3: The amount of data being generated by technology is increasing at an <u>exponential</u> rate.
