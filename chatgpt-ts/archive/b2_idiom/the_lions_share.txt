# Key:the lion's share
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The CEO received <u>the lion's share</u> of the profits while the employees barely got a raise.
E-2: The eldest sibling always got <u>the lion's share</u> of attention from their parents.
E-3: The new restaurant in town has already taken <u>the lion's share</u> of customers from its competitors.
