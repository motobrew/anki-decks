# Key:gist
### English Sentences
E-1: I only caught the <u>gist</u> of the conversation, so I'm not sure about the details.
E-2: The teacher gave us a summary to help us understand the <u>gist</u> of the article.

J-1: 会話の<u>要点</u>しか把握できず、詳細が分かりません。
