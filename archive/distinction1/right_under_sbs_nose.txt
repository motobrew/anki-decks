# Key:right under sb's nose
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The missing keys were <u>right under my nose</u> the whole time.
E-2: He searched for his glasses for hours not realizing they were <u>right under his nose</u>.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 行方不明だった鍵は、ずっと<u>私の鼻の下</u>にあった。
J-2: 彼は眼鏡を探して何時間も過ごしたが、<u>自分の鼻の下</u>にあることに気づかなかった。
