# Key:no offense
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: <u>No offense</u>, but I don't really like sushi.
E-2: <u>No offense</u>, but your idea won't work in practice.
E-3: <u>No offense</u>, but I think your outfit is a bit too flashy.

J-2: 冒頭に<u>失礼</u>しますが、あなたのアイデアは実践には適さないと思います。
J-3: 冒頭に<u>失礼</u>しますが、あなたの服装は少し派手すぎると思います。
