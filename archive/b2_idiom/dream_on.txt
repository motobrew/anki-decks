# Key:dream on
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: You think you can beat me in chess? Dream on!
E-2: I'm going to win the lottery and retire early. Dream on, buddy!
E-3: Maybe one day I'll be a famous pop star. Dream on is what my parents would say.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 私にチェスで負けると思ってる？<u>夢見るな</u>！
J-2: 宝くじで当たって早期退職するつもりだ。おい、<u>夢みろよ</u>！
J-3: いつか有名なポップスターになるかもしれない。だが、両親は<u>夢見るな</u>と言うだろう。
