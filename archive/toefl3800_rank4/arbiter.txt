# Key:arbiter
### English Sentences
E-1: The judge served as an <u>arbiter</u> in the dispute between the two parties.
E-2: The teacher acted as an <u>arbiter</u> to resolve the conflict among the students.

J-1: その判事は2つの当事者の紛争において<u>仲裁人</u>となりました。
