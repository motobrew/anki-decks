# Key:make matters worse
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I was already running late for work and to <u>make matters worse</u>, I spilled coffee on my shirt.
E-2: We were lost in the woods and <u>to make matters worse</u>, it started raining heavily.
E-3: She was sick and couldn't attend the important meeting, and <u>to make matters worse</u>, her laptop crashed.
