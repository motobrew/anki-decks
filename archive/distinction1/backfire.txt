# Key:backfire
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: They thought their joke would be funny, but it ended up <u>backfiring</u>.
E-2: The company's attempt to cut costs ended up <u>backfiring</u> and losing them money in the long run.

J-1: 彼らは冗談が面白いだろうと考えていましたが、結果的には<u>裏目に出ました</u>。
