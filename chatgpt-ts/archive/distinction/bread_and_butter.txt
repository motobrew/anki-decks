# Key:bread and butter
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Making coffee and tea is my <u>bread and butter</u> at this job.
E-2: Selling insurance is his <u>bread and butter</u> and he's very good at it.
E-3: My dad's <u>bread and butter</u> used to be farming before he retired.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: この仕事でコーヒーや紅茶の準備が私の<u>主な業務</u>です。
J-2: 彼の<u>主要な業務</u>は保険の販売で、とても上手です。
J-3: 父の<u>源泉</u>は引退する前は農業でした。
