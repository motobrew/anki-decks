# Key:as far as A is concerned
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: As far as John is concerned, he doesn't want to go to the party tonight.
E-2: As far as grades are concerned, she always earns A's in her classes.
E-3: As far as I am concerned, this is the best pizza in town.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: ジョンに関して言えば、彼は今夜のパーティーに行きたくないと思っています。
J-2: 成績に関して言えば、彼女は常に授業でAの成績を取ります。
J-3: 私に関して言えば、これはこの町で最高のピザだと思っています。
