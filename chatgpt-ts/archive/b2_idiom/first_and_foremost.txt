# Key:first and foremost
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: <u>First and foremost</u>, we need to ensure the safety of our employees.
E-2: <u>First and foremost</u>, we must address the root cause of this problem.
E-3: <u>First and foremost</u>, we should prioritize the needs of our customers.
