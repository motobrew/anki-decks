# Key:gaze at
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: She couldn't help but <u>gaze at</u> the beautiful sunset over the ocean.
E-2: He would often <u>gaze at</u> the stars in the clear night sky.
E-3: The children eagerly <u>gaze at</u> the display of colorful toys in the store window.
