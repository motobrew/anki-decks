# Key:bail sb out
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I need you to <u>bail me out</u> of jail.
E-2: If you have any extra money, can you <u>bail me out</u> and lend me some?
E-3: The company's financial crisis requires a bailout, and the government will <u>bail them out</u>.

J-3: 会社の財政危機は<acronym title="救済措置">bailout</acronym>が必要で、政府が<u>救済する</u>必要があります。
