# Key:tell A apart
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Mary is so good with faces, she can easily <u>tell identical twins apart</u>.
E-2: The labels on the jars are faded, making it difficult to <u>tell the spices apart</u>.
E-3: I can't <u>tell these two songs apart</u>, they sound so similar.
