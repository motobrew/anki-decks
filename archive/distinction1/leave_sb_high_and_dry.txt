# Key:leave sb high and dry
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The company closed down without warning, <u>leaving their employees high and dry</u>.
E-2: He promised to help me move but <u>left me high and dry</u> on moving day.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: その会社は警告なく閉鎖され、社員たちを<u>置き去りにしました</u>。
J-2: 彼は引っ越しの日に手伝うと約束したけど、<u>当日放り出しました</u>。
