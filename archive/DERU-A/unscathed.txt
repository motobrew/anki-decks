# Key:unscathed
## Response

E-1: The car crashed into the tree, but miraculously the driver walked away <u>unscathed</u>.
E-2: Despite the explosion, all of the precious artifacts were found <u>unscathed</u>.
E-3: The athlete managed to finish the race <u>unscathed</u> despite the difficult track.
