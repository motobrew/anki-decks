# Key:sticking point
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: Money has always been a <u>sticking point</u> in our relationship.
E-2: The negotiations have come to a <u>sticking point</u> and it seems like a compromise is impossible.
E-3: Learning how to do calculus was a <u>sticking point</u> for me in college.
