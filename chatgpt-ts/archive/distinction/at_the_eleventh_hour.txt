# Key:at the eleventh hour
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The team was losing the game until their star player scored a goal <u>at the eleventh hour</u>.
E-2: My essay was due at midnight, but I finished it <u>at the eleventh hour</u>.
E-3: The company was close to bankruptcy, but a big investor saved them <u>at the eleventh hour</u>.
