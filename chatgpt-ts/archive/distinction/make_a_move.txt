# Key:make a move
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He's been flirting with her all night, but he still hasn't <u>made a move</u> to ask for her number.
E-2: I think it's time for us to <u>make a move</u> and leave this party before it gets too crazy.
E-3: If you're interested in that job, you should <u>make a move</u> and submit your application before the deadline.
