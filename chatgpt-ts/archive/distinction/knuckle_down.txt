# Key:knuckle down
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: It's time to <u>knuckle down</u> and study for your exams.
E-2: We need to <u>knuckle down</u> and finish this project by the end of the week.
E-3: If you want to succeed, you have to <u>knuckle down</u> and work hard.
