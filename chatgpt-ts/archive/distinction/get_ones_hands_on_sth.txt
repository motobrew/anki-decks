# Key:get one's hands on sth
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I finally <u>got my hands on</u> the new iPhone after waiting in line for hours.
E-2: I've been trying to <u>get my hands on</u> some concert tickets but they're sold out everywhere.
E-3: If you <u>get your hands on</u> that confidential document, be sure to keep it safe.
