# Key:at close quarters
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The two boxers fought <u>at close quarters</u>, exchanging blows in a fierce battle.
E-2: The soldiers had to fight <u>at close quarters</u> with the enemy in a small room.
E-3: The photographer captured stunning shots of wild animals <u>at close quarters</u> in their natural habitats.
