# Key:hardly， before
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: <u>Hardly</u> anyone showed up to the party <u>before</u> midnight.
E-2: I <u>hardly</u> ever used to exercise <u>before</u> I started training for the marathon.
E-3: The company <u>hardly</u> made a profit <u>before</u> the new CEO took over.
