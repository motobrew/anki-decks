# Key:sing sb's praises
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: After Mary won the competition, her coach couldn't stop <u>singing her praises</u>.
E-2: Despite his flaws, his mother always <u>sings his praises</u> to everyone.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: メアリーが競技に勝った後、コーチは彼女のことを<u>絶賛</u>し続けました。
J-2: 彼に欠点はあるが、彼の母親はいつも誰にでも<u>称賛</u>しています。
