# Key:incapacitate
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The injury to his leg would <u>incapacitate</u> him from playing in the game.
E-2: A power outage could <u>incapacitate</u> the entire city's infrastructure.
E-3: The virus can <u>incapacitate</u> the immune system, making the body vulnerable to other infections.

J-3: ウイルスは免疫システムを<u>無力化</u>させ、体を他の感染症に対して脆弱にします。
