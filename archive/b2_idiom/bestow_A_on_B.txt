# Key:bestow A on B
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: The king decided to <u>bestow</u> a title upon his loyal servant.
E-2: The university will <u>bestow</u> an honorary degree upon the renowned scholar.
E-3: The wealthy businessman promised to <u>bestow</u> a large donation upon the charity organization.
