# Key:tell it like it is
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: She's known for <u>telling it like it is>, even if it's not what people want to hear.
E-2: If you want honest advice, ask him. He always <u>tells it like it is>.
E-3: I appreciate when people <u>tell it like it is>, even if it's not easy to hear.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼女は人々が聞きたくないことでも、<u>ズバリ言うことで有名です</u>。
J-2: 正直なアドバイスが欲しいなら、彼に聞いてください。彼はいつも<u>ズバリ言います</u>。
J-3: 簡単に聞こえないことでも、<u>ズバリ言ってくれる人</u>は評価します。
