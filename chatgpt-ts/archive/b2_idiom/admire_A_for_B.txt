# Key:admire A for B
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I <u>admire John</u> for his dedication to his job.
E-2: She <u>admires her grandmother</u> for her wisdom and kindness.
E-3: They <u>admire their coach</u> for his leadership and guidance.
