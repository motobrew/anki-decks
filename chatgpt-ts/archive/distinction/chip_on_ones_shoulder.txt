# Key:chip on one's shoulder
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: He always has a <u>chip on his shoulder</u> and gets defensive when anyone disagrees with him.
E-2: She had a <u>chip on her shoulder</u> after being passed up for the promotion.
E-3: Don't come into the game with a <u>chip on your shoulder</u> or you'll just end up making stupid mistakes.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 彼は常に自分に反対する人がいるとすぐに守りに入るので、<u>傷つきやすい性格</u>だ。
J-2: 彼女は昇進のチャンスを逃した後、<u>言いがかりをつけるようになった</u>。
J-3: ゲームに< u >前向きな姿勢</u >で取り組まないと、愚かなミスをするだけだ。
