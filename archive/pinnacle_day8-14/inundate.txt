# Key:inundate
<h3>English Sentences</h3>
E-1: The heavy rainfall caused the river to <u>inundate</u> nearby houses.
E-2: After winning the lottery, she was <u>inundated</u> with phone calls from long-lost friends.

<h3>Japanese Translation</h3>
J-1: 豪雨によって、川が近くの家を<u>氾濫させました</u>。
J-2: 宝くじに当選した後、彼女は昔の友達からの電話で<u>殺到しました</u>。
