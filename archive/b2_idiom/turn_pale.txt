# Key:turn pale
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: When I saw the ghost, I <u>turned pale</u> and couldn't move.
E-2: After hearing the bad news, she <u>turned pale</u> and fell silent for a moment.
E-3: His face <u>turned pale</u> as he realized he had made a serious mistake.

J-3: 彼は大きなミスをしたことに気付いて、顔が<u>青ざめ</u>ました。
