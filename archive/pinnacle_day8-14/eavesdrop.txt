# Key:eavesdrop
<h3>English Sentences</h3>
E-1: I accidentally <u>eavesdropped</u> on their private conversation at the coffee shop.
E-2: The detective planted a bug in the suspect's room to <u>eavesdrop</u> on their conversations.
