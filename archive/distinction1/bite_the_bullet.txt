# Key:bite the bullet
## Response
### English Sentences
E-1: I know the exam will be difficult, but you just have to <u>bite the bullet</u> and study hard.
E-2: The surgery will be painful, but it's time to <u>bite the bullet</u> and get it over with.

### Japanese Translation
J-1: 試験が難しいことはわかっているけど、<u>覚悟を決めて</u>勉強しなければなりません。
J-2: 手術は痛いだろうけど、<u>覚悟を決めて</u>終わらせるべきです。
